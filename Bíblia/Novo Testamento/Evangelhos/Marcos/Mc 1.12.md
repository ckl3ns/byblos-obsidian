## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the spirit. Gr. pneuma, Mt +3:16. Mt *4:1, etc. Lk *4:1, etc. driveth. Or, “sendeth him forth,” ekballei auton. The expression does not necessarily imply any violence; but seems to intimate the energy of that impulse on our Lord, by which he was inwardly constrained to retire from society.`
