## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`all amazed. ver. 22. Mk 4:41. 7:37. Mt 8:27. 9:33. 12:22, 23. 15:31. Lk 8:25. new doctrine. “new” is kainos, and in this use has a negative connotation. Trench says “kainos may express only the novel and strange, as contrasted, and that unfavorably, with the known and the familiar” (Synonyms, p. 221). Ac 17:19. He 13:9. for. Lk *4:36. 9:1. 10:17-20. spirits. Gr. pneuma, Mt +8:16.`
