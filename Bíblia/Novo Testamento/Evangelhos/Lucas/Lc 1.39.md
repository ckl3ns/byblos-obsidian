`into. ver. 65. Jsh 10:40. 15:48-59. 20:7. 21:9-11. city. This was most probably Hebron, a city of the priests, and situated in the hill country of Judea (Jsh 11:21. 21:11, 13), about 25 miles south of Jerusalem, and nearly 100 from Nazareth. Such was the intense desire of Mary’s mind to visit and communicate with her relative Elizabeth, that she scrupled not to undertake this long journey to effect her purpose.`
