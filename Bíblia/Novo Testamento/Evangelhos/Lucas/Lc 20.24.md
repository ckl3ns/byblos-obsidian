## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a penny. See Mt 18:28mg. 20:2. image. The image was the head of the emperor: the superscription his titles. Julius Caesar was the first who caused his image to be struck on the Roman coin; and Tiberius was the emperor at this time. This therefore was a denarius of Caesar, and consequently this was respecting the tribute required by the Roman government. Caesar’s. ver. 22. Lk 2:1. 3:1. 23:2. Ac 11:28. 25:8-12. 26:32. Ph 4:22.`
