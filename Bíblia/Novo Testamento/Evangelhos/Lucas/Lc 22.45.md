## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`sleeping. Mt *26:40, 43. Mk 13:36. *14:37, 40, 41. for sorrow. Hobart notes this expression as a medical term employed exclusively in Luke’s account, for only Luke assigns a cause for their sleepiness. The other evangelists merely say “their eyes were heavy,” but Luke states that it arose from anxiety. It is evident that their condition was owing to their anxiety for their Lord, coupled with the want of their usual rest (Medical Language, p. 84).`
