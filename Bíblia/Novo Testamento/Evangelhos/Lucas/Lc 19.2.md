## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Zacchaeus. i.e. just, pure, ❅S#2195g. ver. 5, 8. the chief. Arkitelonees, rather, “a chief publican,” or tax gatherer. Probably Zaccheus, who appears from his name to have been a Jew, farmed the revenue of the district around Jericho, having others under him, who either rented of him smaller portions, or were employed as servants to collect the taxes. Lk 3:12. and he. Lk 18:24-27. 2 Ch 17:5, 6. was rich. 1 Ti *6:17.`
