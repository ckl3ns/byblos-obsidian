## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`murmuring. The word in this place seems to mean no more than a private conversation, in a sort of whisper or low voice with differences of opinions and disputes about those opinions, but without displeasure or irritation against Jesus (Scott). This careful attention (almost eavesdropping, Ge +*18:10) is very characteristic of John. ver. 32. Jn 6:41, 61. *9:16. Ph 2:14. some. ver. 25-27, 40-43. Jn +*6:14. 9:16, 17. *10:19-21. Mt 10:25. 16:13-16. *21:46. Lk *7:16. is a good. Mt 19:16. Mk 10:17. Lk *6:45. *18:18, 19. 23:47, 50. Ac 11:24. Ro 5:7. deceiveth. ver. 47, 52. Mt 27:63.`
