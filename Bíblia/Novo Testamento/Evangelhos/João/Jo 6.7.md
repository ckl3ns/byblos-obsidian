## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Two. This sum, rating the denarius at 7 1/2d., would amount to 6l. 5s.; or, reckoning the denarius, with some, at 7 3/4d., it would amount to 6l. 9s. 2d. of our money; which appears to have been more than our Lord and all his disciples were worth of this world’s goods. The denarius was the usual pay for a day’s labor (Mt 20:2, 9, 13). Nu *11:21, 22. 2 K 4:43. Mk 6:37. pennyworth. Jn 12:5. Mt 18:28mg.`
