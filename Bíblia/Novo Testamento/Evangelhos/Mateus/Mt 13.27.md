## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the servants. 1 Co 3:5-9. 12:28, 29. 16:10. 2 Co 5:18-20. 6:1, 4. Ep 4:11, 12. whence. Ro +*16:17. 1 Co 1:11-13. 15:12, etc. Ga 3:1-3. Ja 3:15, 16. *4:4. tares. Zizania, doubtless denotes darnel, the lolium temulentum of the naturalists, a noxious weed which bears a strong resemblance to wheat. “It is well known,” says Mr. Forskal, “to the people of Aleppo. It grows among corn. If the seeds remain mixed with the meal, they occasion dizziness to those who eat of the bread. The reapers do not separate the plant; but, after the threshing, they reject the seeds by means of a van or sieve.” Other travelers say, that, in some parts of Syria, it is drawn up by the hand in the harvest.`
