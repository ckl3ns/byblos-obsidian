## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`If. ƒ60A, Ge +3:22. being. Ge 3:7-11. Ex 32:25. Re *3:18. 16:15. naked. ƒ63C, Ge +25:32. Supply by ellipsis (absolute: brachylogia), “If indeed being clothed also, we shall not be found naked (as some among you say).” There were some among the Corinthians who said “there is no resurrection of the dead” (1 Co 15:12, 35), and here those assertions are thus referred to.`
