## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`To. Is +*8:20n. Ro +*1:7. the saints. The term agios, saint, properly denotes a holy person, separated from sin, and consecrated to God, probably from agos, a thing sacred, purity; and such the gospel requires every man to be, and such every true believer is. To restrict it here to those who adhered to the purity of the Christian faith in opposition to the Judiazing Christians, greatly impoverishes and debases the sense, as Dr. Doddridge well remarks. Taking it in the sense of those few holy persons canonized by the church and now in heaven is of course a wholly mistaken and unscriptural view of the term. See on Ps 16:3. 1 Co *1:2. Ga 3:9.
