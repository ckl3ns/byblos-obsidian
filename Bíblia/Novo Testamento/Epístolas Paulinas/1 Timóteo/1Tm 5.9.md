## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a widow. See on ver. 3, 4. taken. or, chosen. under. ver. 11, 14. Lk 2:36, 37. Ro 16:1. having. 1 Ti 3:2, 12. 1 Co 7:10, 11, 39, 40. wife of one. Some interpreters suggest that this means not having been remarried, not to one husband at a time. Others hold that this refers to a “one-wife husband,” denoting marital faithfulness. Still others, probably more correctly, hold this prohibition excludes from leadership in the church those who have practiced polygamy (Mt ✓19:3n). Ge +*4:19. 1 Co *7:39.`
