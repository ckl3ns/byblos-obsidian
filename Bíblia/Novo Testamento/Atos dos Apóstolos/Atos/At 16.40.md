## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`and entered. ver. 14. Ac 4:23. 12:12-17. the brethren. Who were these brethren? Not Lydia’s children, who were infants (ver. 14n, 15n), and may just as well have been daughters. Rather, as Timothy and Luke accompanied Paul and Silas at this point in Paul’s missionary journey (ver. +10), Paul and Silas returned to Lydia’s home upon being released from prison to rejoin their brethren, Luke and Timothy, who had been staying at Lydia’s home. “Brethren” may also be taken to include those who were appointed “bishops and deacons” at the church of Philippi, who gathered at Lydia’s home. ver. 2.
