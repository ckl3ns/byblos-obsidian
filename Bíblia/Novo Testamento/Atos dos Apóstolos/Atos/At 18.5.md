## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Silas. Ac +15:22. 17:14, 15. 1 Th 3:2, 6. Timotheus. Ac +16:1. were come. This arrival at Corinth of brethren from Macedonia is referred to incidentally in the Epistle (2 Co 11:9), but explicitly here (ver. 1, 5). Silas and Timothy were Paul’s assistants at Corinth (2 Co 1:19). Silas and Silvanus refer to the same person (Ac 17:10. 1 Th 1:1). The differences in the name in the two passages negatives the supposition that the name in the History was transcribed from the Epistle, or the reverse. See related notes (ver. 3, 18. Ac +*12:12n).
