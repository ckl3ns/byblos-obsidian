## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Wealth. Pr 10:2. 20:21. 28:8. Jb 15:28, 29. 20:15, 19-22. 27:16, 17. Ec 5:14. Je 17:11. Hab 2:6, 7. Hg *1:6. Ja 5:1-5. gotten. Charles Bridges regards this as false ellipsis (ƒ63K, Ge 37:13), stating in a footnote to this passage “The interpolation of our translators is uncalled for, and misleads the reader. The word ‘vain’ is of very frequent occurrence, and always implies, not what is sinful, but what is empty and unsubstantial.” Bridges states “This Proverb does not imply the means, by which wealth has been gotten; but the impoverishing use, to which it is applied. However large, by vanity it will soon be diminished” (Commentary, p. 157). he. ver. 22, 23. Pr 20:21. 27:23-27. Ps 128:2. by labor. Heb. with the hand.`
