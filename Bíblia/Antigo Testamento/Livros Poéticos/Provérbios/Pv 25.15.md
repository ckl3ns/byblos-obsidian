## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`long forbearing. Pr *15:1. 16:14. Ge 32:4, etc. 1 S 25:14, 24, etc. Ps 69:20. Ec 10:4. Mt 11:29. Jn 16:22. Ja 1:19. tongue. ƒ121BB, Ps +5:9. By Metonymy of the Cause, “tongue” is put for what is spoken by it, signifying that gentle speech overcomes obstinacy. the bone. Rather, “a bone;” that is, soft and conciliating language will often accomplish the most difficult things.`
