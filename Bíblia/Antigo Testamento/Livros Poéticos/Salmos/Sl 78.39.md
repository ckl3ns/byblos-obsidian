## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`remembered. ƒ22C3, Ge +8:1. flesh. Ps ✓103:14-16. Ge *6:3. Jn *3:6. a wind. Or, as the Hebrew rooach holaich welo yashoov may be rendered, “the spirit goeth away, and returneth not again.” To this purpose the Arabic, “He remembered that they were flesh; and a spirit, which, when it departs, returneth not again.” The human being is composed of flesh and spirit, or body and soul: these are easily separated, and when separated, the body turns to dust, and the spirit returns no more to animate the body in a state of probation. Ps *39:13. Jb *7:7, 16. Ja 4:14.`
