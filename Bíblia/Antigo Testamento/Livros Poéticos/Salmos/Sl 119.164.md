## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Seven times. That is, probably, many times, or frequently, as the term seven denotes (compare Jb 5:19n). Rabbi Solomon says that this is to be understood literally, for they praised God twice in the morning before reading the Decalogue and once after; twice in the evening before the same reading, and twice after; making in the whole seven times. ver. 62. Ps 55:17. Pr 24:16. Da 6:10. Mt 18:21. Lk 17:4. praise. Ps 34:1. because. Ps 48:11. 97:8. Re *15:3. 19:2.`
