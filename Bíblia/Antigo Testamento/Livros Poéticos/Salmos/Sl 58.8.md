## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a snail. Shabbelool, in Chaldee tivlala, the snail, is probably so called from the Arabic balla, to wet, moisten, because of the glutinous slime emitted from its body, by which it appears to waste itself away by its own motion; in the same manner the actions of the wicked prove their own destruction. Nu +*32:23. pass. Ps *37:35, 36. Mt 24:35. Ja 1:10. untimely. Jb *3:16. Ec *6:3.`
