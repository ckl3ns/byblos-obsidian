## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Man, etc. or, Every one did eat the bread of the mighty. Lechem abberim, “bread of the mighty:” they ate such food as could only be expected at the tables of the rich and great; the best, the most delicate food. Or, it might be so called, because it rendered the people healthy, vigorous, and fit for their marches. Ps 103:20. Jn 6:48-50. he sent. Ex 16:8. Mt 14:20. 15:37.`
