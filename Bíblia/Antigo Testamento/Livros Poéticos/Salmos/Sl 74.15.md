`cleave. Ps 105:41. Ex *17:5, 6. Nu *20:11. Is 48:21. fountain. Heb. mayan, Ge +7:11 (❅S#4599h). ƒ121H. Metonymy of the Effect B567: the thing made is put for the material from which it is made or produced. Here, “fountain” is put for the rock from which the fountain flowed. For other instances of this figure see Is 28:28. 33:12. 47:2. flood. Jsh 3:13, etc. 2 K 2:8, 14. Is 11:16. 44:27. Hab 3:9mg. Re 16:12. mighty rivers. Heb. rivers of strength. Ps 24:2. 46:4. 66:6.`
