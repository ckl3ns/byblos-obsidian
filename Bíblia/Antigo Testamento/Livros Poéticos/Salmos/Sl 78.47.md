## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`destroyed. or, killed. ver. 31. with hail. Ps 105:32, 33. Ex 9:18-34. sycamore. From the value of the sycamore in furnishing wood for various uses (see Shaw’s Travels, p. 376, 436), from the grateful shade which its wide-spreading branches afforded, and on account of the fruit, which Mr. Maillet says the Egyptians hold in the highest estimation, we may conceive somewhat of the loss they sustained when “their vines were destroyed with hail, and their sycamore trees with frost.” See Note on 1 Ch 27:28. 1 K 10:27. frost. or, great hailstones.`
