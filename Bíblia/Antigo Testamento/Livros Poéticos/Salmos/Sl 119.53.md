## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Horror. Ps 11:6. La 5:10mg. Zilaphah properly signifies the pestilential burning wind called by the Arabs Simoom (see Ps 11:6). It is here used in a figurative sense for the most horrid mental distress and strongly marks the idea the psalmist had of the corrupting, pestilential, and destructive nature of sin. ver. 136, 158. Ezr *9:3, 14. 10:6. Je 13:17. Da 4:19. Hab 3:16. Lk *19:41, 42. Ac 17:16, 17. Ro 9:1-3. 2 Co 12:21. Ph 3:18. the wicked. 2 P *3:17.`
