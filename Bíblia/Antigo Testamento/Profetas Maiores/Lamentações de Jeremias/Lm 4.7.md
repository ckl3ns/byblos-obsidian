## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Nazarites. Nu *6:2, etc. Jg 13:5, 7. 16:17. Am 2:11, 12. Lk 1:15. purer. 1 S 16:12. Ps 51:7. 144:12. SS 5:10. Da 1:15. than. ƒ102B, Ge +13:16. their polishing. Gizrathom, rendered by Dr. Blayney, “their veining,” from gazar, to divide, intersect, as the blue veins do the surface of the body. This is approved by Dr. A. Clarke, who remarks, “Milk will most certainly well apply to the whiteness of the skin; the beautiful ruby to the ruddiness of the flesh; and the sapphire, in its clear, transcendent purple, to the veins in a fine complexion.”`
