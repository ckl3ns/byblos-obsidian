These incidents happened too long before for them to have been a matter of memory; this furnishes clear evidence that the books in which these events were recorded were extant in Isaiah’s day, not written afterwards. his strange work. ver. 19. Dt 29:21-24. Is 29:13, 14. Je 30:14. La 2:15. 3:33. Ezk 33:21. Hab *3:11. Lk *19:41-44. Ac 13:41. The parallel passages and context show that his “strange work” was the turning of God against his own people.`
