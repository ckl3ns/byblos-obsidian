The enumeration of the parts of a whole which has not been mentioned. One sentence would have expressed the whole, “your sacrifices are not pleasing to me.” But by the figure Synathroesmus, all kinds of sacrifices are enumerated, and the sense is thus amplified and emphasized to show that, with all their outward show of “religion,” there was no true worship of the spirit and heart.
