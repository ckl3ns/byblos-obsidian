## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`An end. This is similar to the second verse, but there is a Paronomasia (ƒ140, Ge 4:25), or play upon the words, here, deserving of notice: keiz ba, ba hakketz, haikeetz ailayich, “the end cometh, come is the end: it waketh for thee.” Ketz is an end; haikeetz is to wake or watch. ver. 3. Je 44:27. watcheth for thee. Heb. awaketh against thee. 2 K +4:31. Zc 13:7. behold. ver. 10. Ezk 21:25. 39:8. 2 P 2:5.`
