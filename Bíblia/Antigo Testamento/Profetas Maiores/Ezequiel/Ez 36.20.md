## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`they profaned. The Jews, when thus scattered, appeared to them an abject and wretched company of people. They were recognized as worshippers of Jehovah wherever they went; but they were looked upon as a viler and more worthless race than any of the idolaters among whom they were driven. Many would ascribe their wickedness to the tendency of their religion, which they abhorred, and not to their acting inconsistently with it; and regard their miseries, not as the punishment of their sins, but as proofs of God’s inability to protect them.
