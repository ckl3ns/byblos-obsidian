## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`to the year. That is, to the year of Jubilee, called the year of liberty, because there was then a general release: all servants had their liberty; and all alienated estates returned to their former owners. Le +*25:10▶𝒫. Mt 25:14-29. Lk 19:25, 26. Ga 4:30, 31. This shows that this prophecy will, and must yet, be literally fulfilled (CB).`
