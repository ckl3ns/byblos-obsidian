## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`with girdles. 1 S 18:4. Is 22:21. all of. Jg 8:18. 2 S 14:25. look to. That is, “princes in appearance;” which seem to have been the deified men worshipped by the Chaldeans. The inhabitants of Judah, like the Israelites, connnected themselves with the Assyrians, and were enamored with their idols; and then with the Chaldeans, and followed their idols; still retaining their attachment to the Egyptians and their idolatrous rites.`
