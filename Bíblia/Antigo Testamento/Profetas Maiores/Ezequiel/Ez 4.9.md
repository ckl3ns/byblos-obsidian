## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`wheat. ver. 13, 16. millet. Dochan, in Arabic dokhu. the holcus dochna of Forskal (Flora Egyptio-arab. p. 174), is a kind of millet, of considerable use as food; the cultivation of which is described by Browne (Travels, p. 291). fitches. or, spelt. Kussemim is doubtless dzea, or spelt, as Aquila and Symmachus render here; and so LXX. and Theodotion, olura. In times of scarcity it is customary to mix several kinds of coarser grain with the finer, to make it last the longer. Ex 9:32. Is 28:25. three. ver. 5.`
