## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Pharaoh shall. Pharaoh, who said he was a god, shall be found among the dead. Ezk +*28:9. shall be comforted. Pharaoh shall console himself, when he sees all these mighty nations and proud conquerors in the same condition as himself. This is spoken according to the common sentiments of mankind, who are better satisfied to suffer with a multitude than alone; though it can be no comfort to those who have perished in their sins, to find multitudes as miserable as themselves (Lk 16:24), Scott. Ezk 14:22. 31:16. La 2:13.`
