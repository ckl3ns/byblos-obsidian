## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`estate. or, place. ver. 7, 21mg. a raiser of taxes in the. Heb. one that causeth an exactor to pass over the, etc. Seleucus Philopater, who levied on his subjects the tribute imposed on his father, and was poisoned by his treasurer Heliodorus. Dt 15:2, 3. 2 K 23:35. glory of the kingdom. ver. 16, 41. The historical fulfillment as this pertained to Palestine is to be found in 2 Maccabees, ch. 3. anger. Heb. angers. Pr 30:33.`
