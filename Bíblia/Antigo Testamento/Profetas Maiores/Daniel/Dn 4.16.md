## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Let his. Here a transition is made from the tree to Nebuchadnezzar, whom it represented; the tree being lost sight of, a person came in its stead. This person having lost the heart, or disposition of a man, and conceiving himself a beast, should act as such, and herd among them. be changed. See on ver. 32, 33. Is 6:10. Hab 1:11. Mk 5:4, 5. Lk 8:27-29. seven times. That is, seven years, a time in the prophetic language denoting a year. ver. 23, 25, 31. Da 7:25. 11:13. 12:7. Re 12:14.`
