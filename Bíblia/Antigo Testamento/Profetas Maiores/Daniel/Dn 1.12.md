## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Prove. Re 2:10. pulse to eat. Heb. of pulse that we may eat, etc. Pulse, zeroim, denotes all leguminous plants, which are not reaped, but pulled or plucked; which, however wholesome, was not naturally calculated to render them fatter in flesh than the others. ver. 16. Ge 1:29, 30. Dt 8:3. Ro 14:2.`
