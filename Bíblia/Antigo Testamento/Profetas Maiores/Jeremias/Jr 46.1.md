## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`The word. This is a general title to the prophecies contained in this and the following chapters, concerning different nations which had less or more connection with the Jews, either as enemies, neighbors, or allies. These prophecies were not delivered at the same time: to some the date is annexed; in others it is left uncertain. against. Je 1:10. 4:7. 25:15-29. Ge 10:5. Nu 23:9. Zc 2:8. Ro *3:29.`
