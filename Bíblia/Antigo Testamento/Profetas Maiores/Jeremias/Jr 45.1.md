The Companion Bible states this “is to be dated 496 B.C., instead of the usually “received” date of 606 B.C. or thereabout” (Ap. 86, p. 124). This appears to be in agreement with Jack Finegan, Handbook of Biblical Chronology, Section 314, pp. 201, 202, and Table 90, p. 202, who places this date at Tishri (Sep/Oct) 606—Elul (Aug/Sep) 605 B.C.
