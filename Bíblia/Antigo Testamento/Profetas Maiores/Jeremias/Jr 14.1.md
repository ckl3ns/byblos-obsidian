## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 3399. B.C. 605. The word. ƒ101, Dt +32:42. This discourse is supposed to have been delivered after the fourth year of Jehoiakim. The Hebrew batzaroth, rendered dearth, signifies restraint, that is, “when the heaven is shut up that there is no rain;” which Houbigant thinks happened early in the reign of Zedekiah. the dearth. Heb. the words of the dearths, or restraints. ƒ121C1L. Metonymy of the Cause. The holding back of rain is put for the famine caused by it. Je 17:8mg.`
