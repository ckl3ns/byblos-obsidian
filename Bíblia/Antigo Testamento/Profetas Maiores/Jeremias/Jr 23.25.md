The false prophets professed that they had revelations from God in supernatural dreams; and they caused this to be rumored abroad. But the Lord declared that it was an imposture: for they only spake their own conjectures, the result of the pride and presumption of their own deceitful hearts. Their purpose, as well as the tendency of their lying predictions, was to seduce the people into forgetfulness of God, and contempt of his word; that they might graft idolatry and superstition on their infidelity, and so render them their attached and submissive followers (Scott). ver. 28, 32.
