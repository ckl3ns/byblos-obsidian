## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`unto the prophet. ƒ121Q1. Metonymy of the Adjunct B597. The appearance of a thing, or an opinion about it, is put for the thing itself, in nouns. Here, Hananiah is probably called a prophet, because he was reputed to be one. ver. 1. For other instances of this figure see Ezk 21:4. Mt 8:12. 9:13. Lk 2:48. 1 Co 1:21, 25. 2 Co 4:4. Ga 1:6. Ti 1:12. Ja 2:14, 17, 20, 24, 26. the house. ver. 1. Je 7:2. 19:14. 26:2.`
