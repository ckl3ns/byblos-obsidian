## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`At that time. Je 7:32-34. 1 K 13:2. 2 K 23:16, 20. 2 Ch 34:4, 5. Ezk *6:4, 5. 37:1, 3. Am 2:1. bring out the bones. This was no uncommon practice at the sacking of cities; and it was the highest expression of hatred and contempt. Horace (Epod. xvi. 11) refers to it, “And her great founder’s hallowed ashes spurn, That slept uninjured in the sacred urn” (Francis). See Note on 2 Ch 34:5n. graves. Heb. qeber, Ge +23:4.`
