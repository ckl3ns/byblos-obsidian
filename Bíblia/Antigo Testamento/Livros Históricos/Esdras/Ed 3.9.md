`Jeshua. Not Jeshua the high priest, before mentioned, but another Jeshua, a Levite, mentioned in the parallel passage. Ezr 2:40. Judah. Hodaviah is called Hodevah by the elision of yood, and was probably named Judah, from the word having the same signification. Ezr 2:40, Hodaviah. Ne 7:43, Hodevah. together. Heb. as one. Ezr 2:64. 6:20. 2 Ch 5:13. Henadad. i.e. grace or favor of Hadad (i.e. chief, most high, Ge +36:35), ❅S#2582h. Ezr 3:9. Ne 3:18, 24. 10:9. (1) One who helped to lay the foundations of the temple, Ezr 3:9. (2) One whose sons helped rebuild the wall of Jerusalem, Ne 3:18. (3) One whose descendants sealed the covenant under Nehemiah, Ne 10:9.`
