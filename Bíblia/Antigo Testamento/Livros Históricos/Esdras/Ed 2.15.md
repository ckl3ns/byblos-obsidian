## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Adin. i.e. ornament; dainty; soft, pliant, pleasant; luxuriant, ❅S#5720h. Ezr 2:15. 8:6. Ne 7:20: 655. 10:16. (1) An Israelite whose descendants returned from Babylon with Zerubbabel, Ezr 2:15; Ne 7:20. (2) One whose posterity came up with Ezra, Ezr 8:6. (3) Probably the same as Number 1. The name of a family who, with Nehemiah and the people, sealed the covenant, Ne 10:16.`
