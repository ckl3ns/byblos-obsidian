## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Nethinims. ver. 58. 1 Ch 9:2. Ne 7:46-56. 10:28. Ziha. i.e. dryness; drought; parching, ❅S#6727h. Ezr 2:43. Ne 7:46. 11:21. (1) A Nethinim whose descendants returned from Babylon, Ezr 2:43; Ne 11:21. (2) A ruler of the Nethinim, Ne 11:21. Hasupha. i.e. made naked; stripped; exhausted; made bare, ❅S#2817h. Ezr 2:43. Ne 7:46. This variation only exists in the translation, the original being written here Husupha, and in the parallel place defectively, Hasupha. Ne 7:46, Hashupha. Tabbaoth. i.e. signets. Ne +7:46.`
