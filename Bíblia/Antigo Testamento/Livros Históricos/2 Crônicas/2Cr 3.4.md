## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the porch. Jn 10:23. Ac 3:11. 5:12. an hundred and twenty. As the height of the temple was only thirty cubits, 120 seems too great a height for the porch; but the Syriac, Arabic, and LXX. in the codex Alexandrinus, have only twenty, probably reading, instead of maiah weesrim, “one hundred and twenty,” ammoth esrim, “twenty cubits,” which brings it within the proportion of the other measures.`
