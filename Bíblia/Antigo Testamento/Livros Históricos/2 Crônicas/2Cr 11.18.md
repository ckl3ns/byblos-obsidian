## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 3029-3046. B.C. 975-958. Mahalath. i.e. sickness; harp, wind instrument; appeasing, ❅S#4258h. 2 Ch 11:18. Ge 28:9. (1) A daughter of Ishmael, whom Esau married, Ge 28:9. (2) Wife of Rehoboam, 2 Ch 11:18. daughter. Eliab was David’s eldest brother; and more than eighty years had elapsed since David, at the age of thirty, began to reign: Abihail must therefore have been grand-daughter to Eliab; and this shows the latitude in which the words son and daughter are used in Scripture. 1 K +15:10n. 2 K 8:26. 1 Ch 7:24n. to wife. Ge +4:19. Eliab. 1 S 16:6. 17:13, 28. 1 Ch 2:13. 27:18, Elihu.`
