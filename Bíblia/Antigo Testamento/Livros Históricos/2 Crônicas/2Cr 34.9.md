## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hilkiah. ver. 14, 15, 18, 20, 22. 2 K 22:4. 23:4. they delivered. 2 Ch 24:11-14. 2 K 22:5-7. Ph 4:8. doors. 1 K +14:17. 2 K 12:9mg. Manasseh. 2 Ch 30:10, 18. 31:1. and they returned. Instead of wyyashuvoo, “and they returned,” as the Keri has, we should, with the Kethiv, read weyoshevey, “and the inhabitants of;” a reading which is supported by many MSS., printed editions, and all the versions, as well as necessity and common sense. ver. 7.`
