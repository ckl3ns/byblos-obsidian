## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 3126-3165. B.C. 878-839. Joash. “Joash” is merely an abbreviation of “Jehoash,” by the elision of hay and here also of wav. 2 K 11:21. 12:1, Jehoash. 1 Ch 3:11. seven years old. As Joash was hidden six years in the temple, and was but seven when he came to the throne, he could have been but one year old when secreted by his aunt.`
