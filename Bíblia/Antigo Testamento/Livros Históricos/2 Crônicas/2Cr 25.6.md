## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`mighty men of valor. 2 Ch 13:3. 17:13. Jg +6:12. an hundred talents of silver. Estimating the shekel at 2s. 6d., and the talent, being about 3000 shekels (see Ex 38:25, 26), at 375l., one hundred talents would amount to 37,500l.; which, divided among 100,000 men, quotes only 7s.6d.; hence we may suppose, that this was only an earnest of their pay, or that they expected to be enriched by the plunder of the Edomites.`
