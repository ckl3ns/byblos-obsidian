## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`they found. Ex 12:35, 36. 1 S 30:19, 20. 2 K 7:9-16. Ps 68:12. Ro 8:37. dead bodies. Instead of pegarim, “dead bodies,” eight MSS. and several ancient editions read begadim, “garments.” None of the ancient versions, except the Chaldee, have dead bodies: garments would therefore appear to be the true reading; and the succeeding clause should be rendered, “which they seized for themselves.” precious jewels. Ex 3:22. Nu 31:51. Jg 8:24-26. Pr 3:15. it was so much. Ezk 39:8, 9.`
