## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Since the day. 2 S 7:6, 7. 1 K 8:16. my name. Ex 20:24. 23:21. Dt 12:5, 11. Da 9:19. neither chose. The judges and Saul were chosen by God, for a season, to be rulers of Israel; but not to establish a permanent and hereditary authority over that people, as was the case with David. This clause is wanting in the parallel passage in Kings; but it helps to clear the sense. 1 S 10:24. 13:13, 14. 15:23. 2 S 7:15, 16.`
