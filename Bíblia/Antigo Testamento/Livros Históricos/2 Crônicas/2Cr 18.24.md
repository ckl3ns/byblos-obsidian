## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Behold. Is 26:11. Je 28:16, 17. 29:21, 22, 32. into an inner chamber. or, from chamber to chamber. Heb. into a chamber in a chamber. “In one of the halls of the seraglio at Constantinople,” says De la Motraye (vol. ii. p. 170), “the eunuch made us pass by several little chambers, with doors shut, like the cells of monks or nuns, as far as I could judge by one that another eunuch opened.” This exactly corresponds with the idea of a “chamber within a chamber;” and it would appear that Michaiah predicted, that Zedekiah should fly for shelter to a Harem, which we have seen was deemed inviolate (See on 1 S 19:16n). 1 K 20:30mg. 22:25mg. 2 K 9:2mg. Is 26:20.`
