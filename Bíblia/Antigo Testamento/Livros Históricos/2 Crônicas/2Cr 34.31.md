## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`in his place. Instead of al omdo, “in his place,” the parallel passage, 2 K 23:3, has al haammood, “by the pillar;” which is probably the true reading, as the LXX. in both places read ton stulon, “the pillar.” 2 Ch 6:13. 2 K *11:14. *23:3. Ezk 46:2. made a covenant. This was expressed, (1) In general, To walk after Jehovah; to have no gods beside him. (2) To take his law for the regulation of their conduct. (3) In particular, To bend their whole heart and soul to the performance of it; so that they might not only have religion without, but piety within. 2 Ch 23:16. 29:10. Ex *24:6-8. Dt 29:1, 10-15. Jsh 24:25. Ne 9:38. 10:29. Je *50:5. He 8:6-13. and his testimonies. Ps 119:111, 112. with all his heart. 2 Ch 15:12, 15. See on 2 Ch *31:21. Dt *6:5. Lk *10:27-29. soul. Heb. nephesh, Ge +34:3. to perform. Ps 119:106.`
