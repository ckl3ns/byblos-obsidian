## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`soldiers of the army. Heb. sons of the band. ver. 9. fell upon the cities. These Israelites seem to have returned home, when discharged by Amaziah, whose powerful army deterred them from attempting revenge at that time; but when he was engaged in war with the Edomites, they marched from Samaria, and plundered all the cities till they came to Beth-horon, where they slew 3000 of the inhabitants. Samaria. 1 K 16:24, 29. Beth-horon. 2 Ch 8:5. Jsh +10:10. 1 K 9:17.`
