## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 3166. B.C. 838. Now it came. 2 K 14:5, etc. established to him. Heb. confirmed upon him. 2 K 14:5. 15:19. he slew. 2 Ch 24:25, 26. Ge *9:5, 6. Ex 21:14. Nu 35:31-33. No doubt those wicked men, Jozachar and Jehozabad, who murdered his father, had considerable power and influence; and therefore he found it dangerous to bring them to justice, till he was assured of the loyalty of his other officers: when this was clear, he called them to an account, and justly put them to death for treason and murder; for, if even these conspirators against Joash intended to avenge upon him the death of Zechariah, they acted without commission from that God “to whom vengeance belongeth.”`
