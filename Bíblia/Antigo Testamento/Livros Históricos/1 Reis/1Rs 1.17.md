## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`My lord. Ge 18:12. 1 P 3:6. thou swarest. It is not recorded when or upon what occasion David sware to Bathsheba that Solomon should succeed him; but it is supposed, with some degree of probability, that it took place after Absalom’s rebellion; and as God himself had settled the succession, he might very properly give her this assurance. ver. 13, 30.`
