## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2989. B.C. 1015. An. Ex. Is. 476. old. David was probably now about sixty-nine years of age. He was thirty years old when he began to reign, reigned forty years, and died in his seventieth year; and the transactions mentioned here are supposed to have taken place about a year before his death. Sixty-nine was not an advanced age; but he had been exhausted with various fatigues, and especially family afflictions, so that he was much older in constitution than in years. 2 S 5:4. 1 Ch 23:1. 29:27, 28. Ps *90:10. Ec 12:1. and stricken in years. Heb. and entered into days. Ge 18:11. 24:1. Jsh 23:1, 2. Lk 1:7. clothes. Ge 24:53. 27:15, 27. heat. Dt 19:6. Ec *4:11. Ezk 24:11.`
