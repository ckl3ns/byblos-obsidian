## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`What cities. ƒ85N. Erotesis; or, Interrogating B954. The asking of questions without waiting for the answer: in disparagements. For another instance of this figure see Is 2:22. my brother. 1 K 5:1, 2. Am 1:9. Cabul. that is, Displeasing, or dirty. Jsh 19:27. Josephus (Ant. l. viii. c.2) says that Cabul, in the Phoenician language, signifies ouk areskon, “displeasing;” and that these cities were situated in the neighborhood of Tyre. Most commentators are persuaded that the city Cabul in the tribe of Asher was one; and probably from this Hiram took occasion to give this name to all the other cities which Solomon had ceded to him. Jsh 19:27.`
