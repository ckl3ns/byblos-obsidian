## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Ruth. Ru 2:10-13. 1 S 25:41. Lk 14:11. handmaid. Not now merely a “maidservant,” as she had called herself in Ru 2:13 (Young). spread therefore. Heb. spread thy wing: the emblem of protection; and a metaphor taken from the young of fowls, which run under the wings of their mother from birds of prey. Even to the present day, when a Jew marries a woman, he throws the skirts of his talith over her, to signify that he has taken her under his protection. ƒ88, Ge +15:15. Ru 2:12. Ge 20:16. Dt 22:30. 27:20. Ezk *16:8. 1 Co 11:5, 6, 10. a near kinsman. or, one that has right to redeem. ver. 12. Ru 2:20.`
