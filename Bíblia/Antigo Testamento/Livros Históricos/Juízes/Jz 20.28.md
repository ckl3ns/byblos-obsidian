## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Phinehas. Nu 25:7-13. Jsh 22:13, 30-32. 24:33. It is evident from this mention of Phinehas, the son of Eleazar, that these transactions must have taken place not long after the death of Joshua. Thus this history did not take place in the chronological order in which it is here placed, after Samson’s death, but long before. See notes on the Figure Hysterologia at Jg 19:1 and 20:1, and the Figure Hysteresis at Jg 18:1. stood. Dt 10:8. 18:5, 7. 2 K 25:8. Pr 22:29. Je 52:12.
