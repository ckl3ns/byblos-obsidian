## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`new. Heb. moist. Is 1:6. jawbone. Dt 18:3. 1 K 22:24. slew. or, smiteth. ver. 16. Jg 3:31. 4:21. 7:16. Le 26:8. Jsh 23:10. 1 S 14:6, 14. 17:49, 50. 1 Co 1:27, 28. a thousand. Some would render the words aileph ish, “a chief”; but it is alluph, and not aileph, which signifies a chief; besides which, the Hebrew idiom would, even in that case, require it to be ish alluph, “a man, a chief,” and not alluph ish, “a chief, a man.” Add to which, that every version renders it “a thousand men.” Jg 3:31. ◐12:6mg,n. 1 Ch 11:11.`
