## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Laish. Jsh 19:47, called Leshem. how they. ver. 27, 28. Re 18:7. magistrate. Heb. possessor, or, heir, of restraint. ver. 28. 1 S 3:13. 1 K 1:6. Ro 13:3. 1 P 2:14. and had no. In the most correct copies of the LXX. this clause reads “and they had no transactions with Syria”; evidently reading instead of adam, “man,” aram, “Syria”; words so nearly similar that the only difference between them is in the Hebrew letter raish and daleth, which in both MSS. and printed books is sometimes indiscernible. Laish was situated on the frontiers of Syria.`
