## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`no king. The word mailech, which generally means a king, is sometimes taken for a supreme ruler, governor, or judge (see Ge 36:31. Dt 33:5); and it is probable it should be so understood here, and in the parallel passages. Jg 17:6. 19:1. 21:25. the tribe. Jsh 19:40-48. for unto. Jg 1:34.`
