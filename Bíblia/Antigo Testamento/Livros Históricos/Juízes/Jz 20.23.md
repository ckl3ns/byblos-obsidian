## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`wept. ver. 26, 27. Ps 78:34-36. Ho 5:15. asked counsel. Is 1:12, 16. Ho 7:14. And the. It seems most evident that the Israelites did not seek the protection of God. When they “went to the house of God” (ver. 18), it was not to enquire concerning the expediency of the war, nor of its success, but which of the tribes should begin the attack: and here the question is, “Shall I go up again to battle against the children of Benjamin my brother?” Having so much right on their side, they had no doubt of the justice of their cause, and the propriety of their conduct; and having such a superiority of numbers, they had no doubt of success. But God humbled them, and delivered them into the hands of their enemies; and showed them that the race was not to the swift, nor the battle to the strong. Ja 4:2.`
