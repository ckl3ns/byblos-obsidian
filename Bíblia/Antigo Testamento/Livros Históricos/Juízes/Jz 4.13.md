## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`gathered. Heb. gathered by cry, or proclamation. Jg 6:34. 1 S 10:17. 2 S 20:4, 5. Jb 35:9. Jon 3:7. Zc 6:8. chariots. ver. 13. Jsh 11:6. nine hundred. See on ver. 2, 3, 7. chariots of iron. ver. 3. Probably chariots armed with iron scythes, projecting from the axle on each side, by which the infantry might be easily cut down or thrown into confusion. The ancient Britons are said to have had such chariots.`
