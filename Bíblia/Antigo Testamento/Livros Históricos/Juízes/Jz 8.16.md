## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the elders. ver. 7. Pr 10:13. 19:29. Ezr 2:6. thorns. Mi 7:4. taught. Heb. made to know. 1 S 14:12. 16:3. Pr 26:3. Ro 13:4. Instead of wyyoda, Houbigant, Le Clerc, and others read wyyadosh, “and he tore or threshed;” and this is not only agreeable to what Gideon threatened (ver. 7), but is supported by the LXX., Vulgate, Chaldee, Syriac, and Arabic. The Hebrew text might easily have been corrupted simply by the change of the letter shin into the letter ayin, letters very similar to each other.`
