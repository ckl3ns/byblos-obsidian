## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`perform. Heb. do. let the king. Esther probably wished another interview, that she might ingratiate herself more fully into the king’s favor, and thus secure the success of her design. But Providence disposed of things thus, to give time for the important event mentioned in the following chapter. tomorrow. Est 6:1, etc. Pr *16:9.`
