## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Yea, Esther. Plutarch, in his life of Artaxerxes, informs us, that none but the king’s mother, and his real wife, were permitted to sit at his table; and therefore he mentions it as a condescension in that prince, that he sometimes invited his brothers. Haman, therefore, had some reason to be proud of this favor. tomorrow. Jb 8:12, 13. 20:5-8. Ps 37:35, 36. Pr 7:22, 23. 27:1. Lk 21:34, 35. 1 Th 5:3.`
