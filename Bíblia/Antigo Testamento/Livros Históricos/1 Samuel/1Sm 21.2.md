## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`The king. The whole of this is a gross falsehood; and which was attended with the most fatal consequences. It is well known that from all antiquity it was held no crime to tell a lie in order to save a life. Thus Diphilon, “I hold it right to tell a lie for safety: nothing should be avoided to save life.” A heathen may say or sing thus: but no Christian can act thus and save his soul, though he may save his life. 1 S ◐16:2n. 19:17. 22:22. Ge 27:20, 24. 1 K 13:18. Ps 119:28, 29. Ga 2:12. Col 3:9.`
