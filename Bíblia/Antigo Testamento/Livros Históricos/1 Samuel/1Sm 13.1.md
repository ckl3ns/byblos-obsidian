## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2911. B.C. 1093. An. Ex. Is. 398. reigned one year. Heb. the son of one year in his reigning. This verse is variously interpreted; but probably it only means, according to the Hebrew idiom, that during the first year nothing remarkable occurred; but after two years, (or in the second year of his reign), the subsequent events took place. Ex 12:5. Mi 6:6mg.`
