## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`cook. ver. +23. the shoulder. Probably the shoulder was set before Saul, not because it was the best part, but because it was an emblem of the government to which he was now called (See Is 9:6). Le 7:32, 33. Ezk 24:4. left. or, reserved. Ge 14:10 (remained). this time. or, appointed season. Young notes, Of the festival, as Ex 13:10.`
