## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`lent him. or, returned him, whom I have obtained by petition, to the Lord. The word hishilteehoo, “I have lent him,” is the Hiphil conjugation of shaal, “he asked,” (ver. 27), and refers to the name of Samuel. Ex 12:36. he shall be. or, he whom I have obtained by petition shall be returned. Ex 3:22. 2 K 6:5. he worshipped. Ge 24:26, 48, 52. 2 Ti 3:15.`
