(1) An Israelite who lived near Kirjath-jearim and in whose house the ark of God was kept after it had been returned by the Philistines, 1 S 7:1; 2 S 6:3, 4. (2) The second son of Jesse, the father of David, 1 S 16:8; 1 Ch 2:13. (3) A son of King Saul, slain at Gilboa by the Philistines with his brother Jonathan, 1 S 31:2; 1 Ch 8:33.
