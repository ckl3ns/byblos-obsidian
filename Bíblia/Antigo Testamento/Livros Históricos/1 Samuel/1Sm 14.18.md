## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Bring hither. The Septuagint reads, “Bring hither the ephod; for he bore the ephod on that day before Israel:” which Houbigant and others think is the true reading. Finding that his son Jonathan and his armor-bearer were absent (ver. +17), Saul wished to consult the high-priest; but the tumult increasing, he says to him, ‘Withdraw thine hand:’ i.e. desist from consulting the ephod on the present occasion; and immediately hastened to make the best use he could of this astonishing victory. 1 S 4:3-5. 30:8. Nu 27:21. Jg 20:18, 23, 28. 2 S 11:11. 15:24-26. For the ark. 1 S 5:2. 7:1.`
