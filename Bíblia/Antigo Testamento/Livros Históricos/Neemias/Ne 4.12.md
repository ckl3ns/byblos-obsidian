## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`ten times. Ge 31:7, 41. Nu 14:22. 1 S +1:8n. Jb 19:3. Young notes, that is, many times. From all places, etc. or, That from all places ye must return to us. ye shall return. Houbigant, Michaelis, and Dathe contend, that instead of tashoovoo, “ye shall return,” we should read chashevoo, “they designed.” they. 63K, Ge +37:13. EWB suggests that this is not a case in which the apodosis is to be supplied, but it may be taken literally, and so read “They said unto us ten times, From all places ye shall return to us” (B117).`
