## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the gate of the fountain. The gate leading either to the fountain of Siloam, on the east of the city, or to that of Gihon, on the west. Ne 3:15. 2 K 18:17n. 20:20. 2 Ch 32:30n. the king’s pool. Probably the aqueduct made by Hezekiah to bring the waters of Gihon to the city of David. 2 S 5:23n. 1 K 1:45n.`
