## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2804, etc. B.C. 1200, etc. in Gibeon. 1 Ch 8:29-40. the father. 1 Ch 2:23, 24, 45, 50-52. Jehiel. i.e. treasured; hidden of God; swept away of God, ✣S#3273h. (1) Father of Gibeon, 1 Ch 9:35. (2) A mighty man of David, 1 Ch 11:44. See on 1 Ch +5:7 for Jeiel (❅S#3273h). whose wife’s. Some editions read achatho, “his sister;” but in the parallel place, 1 Ch 8:29, it is ishto, “his wife,” which is also the reading of the LXX., Vulgate, Arabic, and Syriac here, and is undoubtedly the true reading. This repetition of part of Benjamin’s genealogy seems to have been intended merely as an introduction to the ensuing history.`
