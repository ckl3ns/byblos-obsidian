## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Jeconiah. 2 K 24:6, 8. 25:27. 2 Ch 36:9, Jehoiachin. Je 22:24, 28, Coniah. +24:1. Mt 1:11, Jechonias. Zedekiah. ver. 15. 1 K +22:11. As the sons of Jeconiah are enumerated in the succeeding verse, and as Zedekiah is no where else mentioned as the son of Jeconiah, but as the son of Josiah, it is highly probable that son here means successor. 2 K 24:17, being his uncle. Matthew Poole comments, “Not his natural son, for he was his uncle, 2 K 24:17; but his legal son, or his successor, upon whom the son’s right was devolved by virtue of that law, Nu 27:8-10, and therefore it is not strange if he have the name of son with it.” See 1 K +15:10n.`
