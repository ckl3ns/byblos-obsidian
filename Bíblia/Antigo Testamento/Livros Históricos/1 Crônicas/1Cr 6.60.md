## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Geba. 1 Ch 8:6. Jsh 18:24. 21:17. 1 S +13:3. Alemeth. i.e. covering; a hiding place, ❅S#5954h. Jsh 21:18, Almon. (1) A Levitical city of Benjamin, 1 Ch 6:60, called also Almon, Jsh 21:18. (2) A descendant of Jonathan, son of Saul, 1 Ch 8:36; 9:42. (3) Compare Alameth, 1 Ch 7:8, son of Becher. Almon and Alemeth having the same signification, are perfectly interchangeable. Anathoth. 1 Ch +7:8. 1 K 2:26. Is 10:30. Je 1:1. 11:23. 37:12. thirteen cities. Here there are only eleven enumerated; but two more are added in the book of Joshua—Juttah and Gibeon—which make thirteen. Jsh +10:2. +15:55. ✓21:16, 17. 1 Ch +14:16. None of the versions give the full number of names, though they all give the whole sum thirteen; and it is probable that these two cities had been destroyed and lay in ruins when this book was written, and hence were not enumerated.`
