## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`three years’ famine. In 2 S 24:13, it is seven years; but the Septuagint has there three years, as here; which is, no doubt, the true reading; the letter zayin, seven, being mistaken for the letter gimmel, three.
