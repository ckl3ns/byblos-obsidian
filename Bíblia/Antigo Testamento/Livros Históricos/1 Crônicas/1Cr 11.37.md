## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hezro. i.e. his court; bulwark of the Lord, ❅S#2695h. 1 Ch 11:37. 2 S 23:35. Also 2 S 23:+35, Hezrai. The difference between “Hezrai” and “Hezro” appears to be the result of an ancient scribe’s confusion of the Hebrew letters yood (English i and j) and vaw (English f). Carmelite. 1 S 30:5. Naarai. i.e. child of the Lord; my boys; my shakings; my roarings, ❅S#5293h. 2 S 23:35, Paarai the Arbite. Ezbai. i.e. spoil; my humblings, ❅S#229h.`
