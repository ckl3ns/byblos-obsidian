## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Sibbecai. i.e. thicket of Jah; my thickets, ❅S#5444h. 1 Ch 11:29. 20:4. 27:11. 2 S 21:18. The reading of “Mebunnai,” for “Sibbecai,” seems to be occasioned by the mistake of a samech for a mem, and a noon for a caph; and a difference in the vowel points. 1 Ch 27:11. 2 S 23:+27, Mebunnai. Ilai. i.e. supreme; most high; my elevations; my sucklings, ❅S#5866h. 2 S 23:+28, Zalmon. Ahohite. ver. 12.`
