## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hadarezer. “Hadadezer” in the parallel passage, seems an evident mistake for “Hadarezer,” for the LXX. and Vulgate there, as here, read “Adarezer.” The difference arises from the mistake of a raish for a daleth, two letters very similar. 2 S 8:3, Hadadezer. Zobah. 1 S +14:47. 2 S 10:6. Ps 60, title. by the river. Ge 15:18. Ex 23:31.`
