## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Ozem. i.e. strength; eagerness, ❅S#684h. 1 Ch 2:15, 25. (1) The sixth son of Jesse and brother of David, 1 Ch 2:15. (2) One of the sons of Jerahmeel, 1 Ch 2:25. David. It appears from the parallel places of Samuel (1 S 16:10), that Jesse had eight sons, of whom David was the eighth and youngest; but one may have died before David came to the throne. 1 S 16:10, 11. 17:12-14.`
