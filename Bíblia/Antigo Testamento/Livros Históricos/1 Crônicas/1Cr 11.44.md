## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Uzzia. i.e. strength of God; power of God, ❅S#5814h. Ashterathite. i.e. a native of Ashtaroth (i.e. queen of heaven; thought searching). Jsh 9:10. Shama. i.e. a hearkener; hearing, ❅S#8091h. Jehiel. i.e. removed of God. 1 Ch +9:35. Hothan. i.e. signet-ring, ❅S#2369h. 1 Ch 7:+32, Hotham. 11:44. Some regard “Hothan” to be an incorrect spelling of “Hotham.” Aroerite. i.e. gentilic of Aroer (i.e. destitute; naked; a naked tree, or heath, Nu 32:34); a native or inhabitant of Aroer (1 S 30:28), ❅S#6200h.`
