## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`fled before Israel. ver. 13, 14. Ps 18:32. 33:16. 46:11. seven thousand. In the parallel passage, “the men of seven hundred chariots;” which difference probably arose from mistaking noon final, which stands for 700, for zayin, with a dot above, which denotes 7000, or vice versa: the great similarity of these letters might easily cause the one to be mistaken for the other. footmen. If these troops were as we have supposed, a kind of dismounted cavalry, the terms footmen and horsemen might be indifferently applied to them. 2 S 10:18, horsemen.`
