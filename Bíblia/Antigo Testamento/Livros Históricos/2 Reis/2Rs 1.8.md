## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`an hairy man. That is, he wore a rough garment, either made of camels’ hair, as that of John the Baptist, or of a skin, dressed with the hair on. Sir J. Chardin informs us, in a MS. note on this place, cited by Mr. Harmer, that the eastern dervishes and fakeers are clothed just as Elijah was, with a hairy garment, girded with a leathern girdle. Is 20:2. Zc 13:4mg. Mt 3:4. 11:8. Lk 1:17. Re 11:3.`
