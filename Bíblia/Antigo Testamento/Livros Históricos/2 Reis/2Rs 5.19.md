If Elisha had said, “Yes, you may bow,” that would have been to sanction idolatry. And if he had said, “No, you must not bow,” that would have been to put Naaman’s conscience under a yoke of bondage to Elisha (EWB, B804). For other instances of this figure see Ezk 12:13. Jn 19:22.
