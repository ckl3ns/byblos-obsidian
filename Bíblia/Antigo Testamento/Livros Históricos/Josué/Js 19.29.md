## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Ramah. i.e. the high place. Different from that in ver. 36. Probably the Rama mentioned by Theodoret as a city of Syria; and placed in some maps between Sarepta and Sidon, eastward, near Lebanon. strong city. or, fenced city. Nu 13:19. 2 S *24:7. Tyre. Heb. Tzor. i.e. a rock. 2 S 5:11. 24:7. 1 K 5:1. 7:13. 9:11, 12. 1 Ch 14:1, 2. 2 Ch 2:3, 11. Ps 45:12. 83:7. Is ch. 23. Ezk ch. 26–28. Some think it another place of the same name. Hosah. i.e. a refuge. Achzib. i.e. a lie. As in Jg 1:31. Different from Jsh 15:44; Mi 1:14. Ge 38:5.`
