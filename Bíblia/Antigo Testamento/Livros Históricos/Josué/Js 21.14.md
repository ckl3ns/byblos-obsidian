## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Jattir. Jattir or Jether, according to Eusebius, was situated in the district of Daroma, or the southern part of Judah, 20 miles south from Eleutheropolis, towards the city of Malatha. Jsh 15:48. 1 S 30:27, 28. Eshtemoa. Eusebius says Eshtemoa, or Esthema, was a great city in the south of Judah, and in the district of Eleutheropolis, north of the city. Jsh 15:50, Eshtemoh. 1 S 30:26, 28. 1 Ch 4:17, Eshtemoa. 6:57.`
