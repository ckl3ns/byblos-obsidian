## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Debir. Debir was situated in the south of the tribe of Judah, near Hebron. The expression of Joshua’s “returning” to Debir probably denotes that having carried his conquests in the southern part as far as Gaza (ver. 41), which was in the southwest angle of Canaan, he then marched back to besiege Debir. Jsh 12:13. 15:15-17, 49. 21:15. Jg 1:11-15.`
