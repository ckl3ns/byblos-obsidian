## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`to the brink. Heb. to the extremity. i.e. so far as the river then spread itself, which was now more than ordinary, ver. 15. command. ver. 3. 1 Ch 15:11, 12. 2 Ch 17:8, 9. 29:4-11, 15, 27, 30. 30:12. 31:9, 10. 35:2-6. Ne 12:24-28. 13:22, 28. ye shall stand. ver. 17. Ex 14:13. La 3:26. in. This expression signifies not the exact middle of a place, but any part within it, as appears from Ge 45:6. Ex 8:22. 24:18. Jsh 7:13. 10:13. Pr 30:19. Thus, within the waters of Jordan, in the first entrance into the river; where they stood for a season, till the river was divided, and then they went into the midst of it, as is implied, ver. 17, and there abode till all the people were passed over, as it follows in the history (Matthew Poole).`
