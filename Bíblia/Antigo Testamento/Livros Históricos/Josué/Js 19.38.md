## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Iron. i.e. the little fearful one, ❅S#3375h. Migdal-el. i.e. tower of God, ❅S#4027h. Probably the Magdala of Mt 15:39. Horem. i.e. devoted; banned; separated; dedication, ❅S#2765h. Beth-anath. i.e. the house of answers, responses, replies, echoes, ❅S#1043h. Jg 1:33, 33. Not the same place as Jsh 15:59. Eusebius mentions a town of the name of Batanaian, fifteen miles from Caesarea (Diocaesarea or Sephoris, probably). Beth-shemesh. i.e. house of the sun. Different place from that in ver. 22. Jsh 21:16. Jg 1:33.`
