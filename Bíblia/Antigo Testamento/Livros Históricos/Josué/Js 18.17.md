## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`En-shemesh. The fountain of the Sun; whether a town, or simply a fountain, is uncertain. Jsh 15:7. Geliloth. i.e. borders, ❅S#1553h. Jsh 15:7. Geliloth is probably the same as Gilgal; though as the word may signify borders or limits, some think that it is probably not the proper name of a place: “And went forth towards the borders which are over against the ascent to Adummim.” Others render Geliloth circuits or roundings, or the hills about Jordan. the stone. Jsh 15:6.`
