## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Giblites. i.e. stone-squarer, see 1 K 5:18mg. Ps 83:7. Ezk 27:9. ❅S#1382h. 1 K 5:18h. Probably the inhabitants of the country around Gebal (Ezk 27:9), or Byblos, as the LXX. render, a city of Phoenicia, situated on the Mediterranean, between Sidon and Tripoli, on the north of the river Adonis. Its walls are about a mile in circumference, with square towers about every forty yards’ distance. Anciently it must have been a place of no mean extent and of considerable beauty, from the ruins still visible. Lebanon. Dt 1:7. 3:25. Baal-gad. Jsh 12:7. under mount. Jsh 11:17. unto the. Nu 34:8. Is 10:9. Am 6:2.`
