## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`in their. Heb. on their heap. The Vulgate, Syriac, Onkelos and Waterland render al tillom, “on their hills.” As the cities of the plain might be easily attacked and carried, Joshua destroyed them; but as those on mountains, hills, or other eminences, might be retained by him with little trouble, prudence would dictate their preservation. Je 30:18.`
