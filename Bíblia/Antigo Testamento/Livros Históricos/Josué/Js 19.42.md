## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Shaalabbin. i.e. place of foxes, ❅S#8169h. Jg 1:35, Shaalbim. 1 K 4:9, Shaalbim. Eusebius calls it Salaba, and places it in Samaria: and Jerome calls it Salebi (Ezk 48), and joins it to Ajalon and Emmaus. Ajalon. i.e. little hind. This appears to be the Ajalon which Jerome places two miles from Nicopolis or Emmaus, in the road to Jerusalem. Jsh *10:12. 21:24. Jg 1:35. 1 S 14:31. 1 Ch 6:69. Jethlah. i.e. lofty place, ❅S#3494h.`
