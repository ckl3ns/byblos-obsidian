`Zanoah. i.e. marsh; a rejected place, ❅S#2182h, only here. ver. 56. 1 Ch 4:18. Ne 3:13. 11:30. (1) In 1 Ch 4:18 Jekuthiel is called “the father of Zanoah,” that is, he was the founder of a village of that name, a town in the hill country of Judah, Jsh 15:56. (2) A town in the lowlands of Judah, Jsh 15:34; Ne 3:13; 11:30. En-gannim. i.e. fountain of gardens. Tappuah. i.e. an apple. ver. 53. Jsh 12:17. 16:8. 17:8. Enam. i.e. fountains; double fountain, ❅S#5879h.`
