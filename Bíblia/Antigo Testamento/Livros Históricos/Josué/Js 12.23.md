## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Dor. i.e. habitation, circuit, circle; generation; dwelling, ❅S#1756h. Jsh 11:2. 17:11. Jg 1:27. 1 K 4:11. 1 Ch 7:29. the nations. Ge 14:1, 2. Is 9:1. Gilgal. Jsh 4:19. 5:9, 10. Not of that Gilgal where Joshua first lodged after his passage over Jordan, where it doth not appear that there was either king or city; but of another city of the same name (as was frequent in those parts: see Jsh 12:9n), probably in Galilee towards the sea, whither divers people might possibly resort for trade and merchandise, over whom this was king, as formerly Tidal seems to have been, Ge 14:1 (Matthew Poole).`
