## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`That ye come. Have no civil or social contracts with them, as these will infallibly lead to spiritual affinities—in consequence of which, ye will make honorable “mention of the name of their gods,” “swear by them,” and “serve them” in their abominable rites; and “bow yourselves unto them,” as your creators and preservers. All this will follow by simply coming among them. He who walks in the counsel of the ungodly, will soon stand in the way of sinners, and sit in the seat of scorners. ver. 12. Ex 23:33. Dt 7:2, 3. Ps 119:115. Pr 4:14, 15. 1 Co 15:33. 2 Co *6:14-17. Ep *5:11. neither. Ex *23:13. Nu 32:38. Ps *16:4. Ho 2:17. to swear. Je 5:7. Zp 1:5.`
