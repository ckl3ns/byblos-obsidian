`I will raise. 2 S 13:1-14, 28, 29. 15:6, 10. 16:11. I will take. That is, in the course of my providence I will permit this to be done. Such phrases in Scripture do not mean that God either does or can do evil himself; but only that he permits such evil to be done as he foresaw would be done, and which, had he pleased, he might have prevented. 2 S 16:21, 22.
