## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`And David. David, intending no doubt to punish Joab, and to lessen his authority with the people, commanded him to take upon him the office of chief mourner; but, as his revenge was gratified, his rival removed, and no heavier punishment inflicted, it is probable his hardened mind would feel but little objection to the ceremony. Rend. 2 S 1:2, 11. Ge 37:29, 34. Jsh 7:6. Jg 11:35. 2 K 19:1. bier. Heb. bed. Lk 7:14.`
