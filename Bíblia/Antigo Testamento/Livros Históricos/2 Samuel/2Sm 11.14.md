## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`wrote a letter. It was resolved in David’s mind that Uriah must die—that innocent, valiant, and gallant man, who was ready to sacrifice his life for the honor of his prince; and, worst of all, by being himself made the bearer of letters to Joab which prescribed the mode by which he was to be murdered. This was the greatest treachery and villany on the part of David; while Joab appears to enter as fully upon the execution of the murder, being perhaps pleased to have this opportunity of further enthralling his king and thus increasing his own power. 1 K 21:8-10. Ps 19:13. 52:2. 62:9. Je 9:1-4. 17:9. Mi 7:3-5.`
