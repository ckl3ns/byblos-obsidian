## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`What hast. Joab and his brother Abishai, David’s nephews, had been very faithful and highly useful to him in his distresses; and from gratitude and natural affection, he had inadvertently permitted them to assume almost as much ascendency over him as Abner had over the pusillanimous Ishbosheth: he trusted and feared them too much, and allowed them all the importance they claimed; which had emboldened them, especially Joab, to a high degree of presumption. ver. 8, 39. 2 S 19:5-7. Nu 23:11. Jn 18:35.`
