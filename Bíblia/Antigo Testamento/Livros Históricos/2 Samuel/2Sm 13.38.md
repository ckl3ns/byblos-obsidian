## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2974-2977. B.C. 1030-1027. An. Ex. Is. 461-464. Geshur. This was not the Geshur lying between Philistia and Egypt (Jsh 13:13. 1 S 27:8), but another in Syria; probably the same as that beyond Jordan, whose inhabitants are joined with those of Maachathi, Dt. 3:14. Jsh 12:5. 2 S 3:3. 14:23, 32. 15:8. three years. Until his recall, as in 2 S 14:23 (Young).`
