## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Baanah. i.e. son of response; answering; affliction; son of grief, ❅S#1196h. ver. 2, 6, 9. 2 S 23:29. 1 Ch 11:30. Ezr 2:2. Ne 7:7. 10:27. (1) The father of one of David’s valiant men, 2 S 23:29; 1 Ch 11:30. (2) One of Ishbosheth’s captains, 2 S 4:2, 5, 6, 9. (3) An Israelite who returned from Babylon, Ezr 2:2; Ne 7:7. (4) Perhaps same as number 3, Ne 10:27. went. 2 Ch 24:25. 25:27. 33:24. lay on a bed. It is customary, in all hot countries, to travel or work very early and very late, and to rest at noon, at which time the heat most prevails. 2 S 11:2. 1 K 16:9. Pr 24:33, 34. 1 Th 5:3-7.`
