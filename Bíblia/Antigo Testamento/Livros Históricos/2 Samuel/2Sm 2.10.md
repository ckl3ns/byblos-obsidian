## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`two years. Houbigant proposes to read shesh shanah, “six years,” instead of shetayim shanim, “two years,” of the text, which he contends is a solecism; for, in pure Hebrew, the words should be shetayim shanah; and this is the reading of twenty MSS.; but “two” is acknowledged by all the versions and MSS. yet collated. Judah followed. 1 K 12:20.`
