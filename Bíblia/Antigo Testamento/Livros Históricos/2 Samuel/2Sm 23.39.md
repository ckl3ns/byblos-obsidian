## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Uriah. 2 S 11:3, 6, etc. 12:9. 1 K 15:5. 1 Ch 11:41. Mt 1:6. thirty and seven in all. From the number of these officers being thirty-seven, it is almost self-evident that shalishim cannot denote “the thirty,” as rendered in ver. 13, etc., but some particular description of men, or officers; for it can scarcely be said, with propriety, that we have thirty-seven out of thirty; and besides, in the parallel place in 1 Chronicles, there are sixteen added!`
