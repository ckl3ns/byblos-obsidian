## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Ira. i.e. stirring; watchfulness, ❅S#5896h. 2 S 23:26, 38. 1 Ch 11:28, 40, Ithrite. 27:9. (1) One of David’s chief rulers after the rebellion of Sheba was quelled, 2 S 20:26. (2, 3) Two of David’s valiant men, 2 S 23:26, 38; 1 Ch 11:28, 40; 27:9. Jairite. ❅S#2972h, only here. Nu 32:41. Jg 10:4, 5. chief ruler. or, prince. 2 S 8:18. Ge 41:43, 45. Ex 2:14, 16. Is +24:2mg. The Hebrew is cohen ledawid, which might be rendered, “a priest of David;” and so the Septuagint, Vulgate, Syriac, and Arabic. The Chaldee has rav, a “chief” or “prince;” probably he was a kind of domestic chaplain or seer to the king. See 2 S 24:11. 2 Ch 35:15.`
