## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Absalom fled. As Absalom had committed willful murder, he could not avail himself of a city of refuge but went to Talmai, king of Geshur, his maternal grandfather. Talmai. 2 S 3:3. 1 Ch 3:2. Ammihud. i.e. my people is honorable; people of majesty, ❅S#5989h. or, Ammihur. Nu 1:10. 2:18. 7:48, 53. 10:22. 34:20, 28. 2 S 13:37. 1 Ch 7:26. 9:4. (1) An Ephraimite whose son was appointed by the Lord chief of the tribe, Nu 1:10; 1 Ch 7:26. (2) A Simeonite whose son Shemuel was appointed to divide the land, Nu 34:20. (3) A man of Naphtali whose son Pedahel was appointed to divide the land, Nu 34:28. (4) Father of Talmai, king of Geshur, 2 S 13:37. (5) A man of Judah, a descendant of Pharez, 1 Ch 9:4.`
