## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a hot burning. Heb. a burning of fire. Is 3:24. He 12:5, 11. 1 P =4:12. This is supposed to state the case of such as had been hurt by fire; which would leave a scar, in which the leprosy might appear, and which was to be distinguished by the rules here given.`
