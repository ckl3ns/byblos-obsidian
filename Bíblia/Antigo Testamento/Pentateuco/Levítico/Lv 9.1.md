## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the eighth day. Not on the eighth day of the month, but on the first day after their consecration, which occupied seven days, and before which they were deemed unfit to minister in holy things, being considered in a state of imperfection. All creatures, for the most part, were considered as in a state of uncleannesss and imperfection, seven days, and perfected on the eighth (see Le 12:2, 3. 14:8-10. 15:13, 14. 22:27. Nu 6:9, 10). Le 8:33. 14:10, 23. 15:14, 29. Nu 6:10. Ezk 43:26, 27. Mt 28:1. Col =3:4.`
