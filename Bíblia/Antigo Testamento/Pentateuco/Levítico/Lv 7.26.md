## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`ye shall eat no. The prohibition of the fat or suet, which was restricted to animals offered in sacrifice, taught reverence to the altar and ordinances of God; but that of blood, which was extended to all land animals, had special respect to the atoning blood of the sacrifice, and of the great antitype which the sacrifice prefigured. Le 3:17n. 17:10-14. Ge 9:4. Nu 33:25. 1 S 14:33, 34. Ezk 33:25. Jn 6:53. Ac 15:20, 29. 21:25. Ep 1:7. 1 Ti 4:4.`
