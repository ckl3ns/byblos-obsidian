Ex 31:13. Ezk 20:12. 46:1. Ac 15:21. He *4:3-5, 9. Young states “This is the first of the ‘appointed seasons of Jehovah.’ It is here said to be a ‘holy convocation,’ which shows or implies that it was the design of God that they should have stated meetings on that day; and it need not be doubted but that these were for the reading of the Law, and the worship of God, by the priests, and especially the Levites, who are everywhere regarded as the instructors of Israel (see on 2 Ch 35:3n); compare 2 K 4:23, etc.”`
