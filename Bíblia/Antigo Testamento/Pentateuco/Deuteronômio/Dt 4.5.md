## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`I have taught. ver. 1. Pr 22:19, 20. Mt 28:20. Ac 20:27. 1 Co 11:23. 15:3. 1 Th 4:1, 2. He 3:5. The people had been often ready to conclude that Moses taught them by his own authority; but at the close of his life he solemnly assured them that he had instructed them exactly as the Lord had commanded him, neither more, nor less, nor otherwise. This is a most express declaration that he was divinely inspired, and utterly incompatible with his integrity of character, if he was not (Scott).`
