## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`save, etc. or, To the end that there be no poor among you. ver. 11. Houbigant follows this marginal reading, to which he joins the end of the third verse, considering it as explanatory of the law; as if he had said, “Thou shalt not exact the debt that is due from thy brother, but thy hand shall release him, for this reason, that there may be no poor among you through your severity.” He justly contends that the phrase ephes kee, can here only mean, to the end that. greatly bless. See on Dt 14:29. 28:1-8, 11. Pr 11:24, 25. 14:21. 28:27. Is 58:10, 11.`
