## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Sheba. i.e. an oath. therefore. Ge 21:31. Beer-sheba. i.e. the well of the oath. ❅S#7656h. ver. 28. This may have been the same city which was called Beer-sheba a hundred years before this, in the time of Abraham; but as the well, from which it had its name originally, was closed up by the Philistines, the name of the place might have been abolished with the well; when, therefore, Isaac reopened it, he restored the ancient name of the place. unto this day. Ge +19:38. Dt +29:4.`
