## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`scattered. ver. 4, 9. Ge 49:7. Dt 32:8. Ps *92:9. Is *8:9. Lk *1:51. Ac 8:1. upon. Ge 10:25, 32. face. ƒ144A1. Figure of speech Pleonasm; or, Redundancy B406: two nouns are used together, one of which appears to be redundant, but is used to emphasize and enhance the force of the other noun; here, “face” emphasizes ‘all over the earth.’ Sometimes the word “face,” though present in Hebrew, is omitted in translation.
