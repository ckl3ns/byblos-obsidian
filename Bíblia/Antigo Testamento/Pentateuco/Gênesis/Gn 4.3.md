## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 129. B.C. 3875. in the process of time. Heb. at the end of days. Either at the end of the year, or of the week, i.e. on the Sabbath. Daniel Wilson notes “as only one division of days had been mentioned, that of the days of the week, the Sabbath being the last or seventh of them—we may reasonably suppose that holy season to be termed ‘the end of the days,’ Ge 2:2 (The Lord’s Day, p. 18). See related notes (Ge 2:3n. 29:27n. Ex 16:26n. Is 58:13n). Ge +*24:55mg. Le 25:29. Jg 11:40mg. 1 K 17:7mg, 15mg. Ne +13:6mg. the fruit. Ge *3:17. Le 2:1-11. Nu 18:12. Dt ◐26:2. Ro 3:28.`
