## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Duke Korah. As it is certain from ver. 4, that Eliphaz was Esau’s son by Adah, and from ver. 11, 12, that Eliphaz had but six sons, ‘Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, Kenaz, and Amalek;’ as it is also certain, from ver. 5, 14, that Korah was the son of Esau (not Eliphaz) by Aholibamah; and as the words duke Korah are omitted by both the Samaritan Text and Version, Dr. Kennicott pronounces them to be an interpolation. dukes. Ex 15:15.`
