## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the generations. Toledoth, the history, narrative, or account of the lives and actions of Jacob and his sons; for in this general sense the original must be taken, as in the whole ensuing history there is no genealogy of Jacob’s family. Ge 2:4. 5:1. 6:9. 10:1. 11:10, 27. 25:12, 19. 36:1, 9. feeding. Jn =10:11, 14. wives. Ge 30:4, 9. 35:22, 25, 26. evil report. 1 S 2:22-24. Jn =3:19, 20. 7:7. 1 Co 1:11. 5:1. 11:18.`
