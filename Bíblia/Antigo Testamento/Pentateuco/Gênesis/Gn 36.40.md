## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Second aristocracy of dukes, from A.M. cir. 2471, B.C. cir. 1533; to A.M. cir. 2513, B.C. cir. 1491. And these. ver. 31. 1 Ch 1:51-54. dukes. See on ver. 15, 16. Ex 15:15. 1 Ch 1:51-54. Timnah. i.e. portion assigned, ❅S#8555h. ver. 12, 22. 1 Ch 1:36, 39, 51. (1) The concubine of Eliphaz, the son of Esau, Ge 36:12, 22; 1 Ch 1:39. (2) One of the chiefs of Edom descended from Esau, Ge 36:40; 1 Ch 1:51. (3) A son of Eliphaz, the son of Esau, 1 Ch 1:36. (4) Timnah, i.e. portion assigned, ❅S#8553h, A town in the north border of Judah, Jsh 15:10; 2 Ch 28:18. It is probably the same as Timnath, number 1. (5) A town in the mountains of Judah, Jsh 15:57. Alvah. or, Aliah. i.e. iniquity, ❅S#5933h. 1 Ch 1:51. Jetheth. i.e. nail; subjugation, ❅S#3509h. 1 Ch 1:51.`
