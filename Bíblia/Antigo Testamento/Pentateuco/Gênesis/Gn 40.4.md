`the captain. Ge 37:36. 39:1, 21-23. Ps 37:5. served. Lk =22:27. a season. Heb. Yamim, literally days; how long is uncertain, though the word may signify, as many suppose, a complete year (see Ge 4:3. 24:55); and as Pharaoh called them to an account on his birthday (ver. 20), Calmet supposes they had offended on the preceding birthday, and thus had been one whole year in prison. ƒ171T2C, Ge +24:55.`
