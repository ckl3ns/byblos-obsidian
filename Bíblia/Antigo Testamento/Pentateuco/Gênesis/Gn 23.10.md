## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`dwelt. Or, sitting (as the word frequently denotes) among the children of Heth (Hittites), at the gate of the city, where all public business was transacted. Ephron, though a chief man, might have been personally unknown to Abraham; but now he answers for himself, making a free tender of the field and cave to Abraham, in the presence of all the people, which amounted to a legal conveyance to the Patriarch. audience.
