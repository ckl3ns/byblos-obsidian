`towns. or, villages. Is 42:11. castles. or, encampments. Nu 31:10. Ps 83:6. The word tiroth, rendered castles, is supposed by some to denote here towers, fortified rocks, or mountain tops, and fastnesses of various kinds in woods and hilly countries; but it rather means, shepherds’ cots, surrounded by sufficient enclosures to prevent the cattle from straying, as the cognate Syriac word teyaro, and Arabic tawar, signify a sheep fold. 1 Ch 6:54. Ne 8:16. twelve. Ge 17:20, 23. nations. Nu 25:15. Ps 117:1.`
