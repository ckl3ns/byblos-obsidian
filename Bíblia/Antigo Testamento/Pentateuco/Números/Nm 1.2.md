## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Take ye the sum. This numbering was probably intended to illustrate the Divine faithfulness in thus increasing the seed of Abraham; to prepare them to preserve due order in their march; and to distinguish the tribes and families. Nu 26:2-4, 63, 64. Ex 30:12. 38:26. 2 S 24:1-3. 1 Ch 21:1, 2. 27:23, 24. the children. Ge 49:1-3. Ex 1:1-5. after. ver. 18, 22, 26, etc. Ex 6:14-19.`
