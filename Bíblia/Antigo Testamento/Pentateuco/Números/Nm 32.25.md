## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`spake. ƒ96D5. Heterosis of Person and Number B525: the singular for the plural. Here, “spake” is singular: he spake, and is put for the plural, they spake. As this figure is not directly observable in English translation, it is unmarked at the following passages where instances of this figure occur: 1 S 16:4. Est 9:23. Jb 12:7. Ps 73:7. Pr 14:1, 9. Thy servants. Jsh 1:13, 14.`
