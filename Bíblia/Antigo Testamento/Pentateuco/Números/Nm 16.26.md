## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Depart, I pray you. The rebels, with all that belonged to them, were, as an accursed thing, devoted to utter destruction (Le 27:28, 29. Jsh 7:13-15, 23-26). The people therefore were forbidden to touch any thing belonging to them; that they might enter a solemn protest against their wickedness, acknowledge the justice of their punishment, and express their fear of being involved in it. See on ver. 21-24. Ge 19:12-14. Dt 13:17. Is 52:11. Je 51:6. Mt 10:14. Ac 2:40. 8:20. 13:51. 2 Co 6:17. 1 Ti 5:22. Re 18:4.`
