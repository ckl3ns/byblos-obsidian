## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`they departed. Nu 12:16. Rithmah. i.e. binding; broom copse, ❅S#7575h. Rithmah was a place in the wilderness of Paran, near Kadesh Barnea; probably so called from the great number of juniper trees, as the name signifies, growing in that district. Not mentioned elsewhere, some suppose it the same as Kadesh-Barnea. Nu 2:16. 13:1, 26. 32:8. Dt 2:19. Jsh 14:7.`
