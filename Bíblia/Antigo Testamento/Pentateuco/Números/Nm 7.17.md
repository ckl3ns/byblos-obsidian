## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`peace offerings. Le ch. 3. +*23:19. 2 Co 5:19-21. this was the offering. It is worthy of remark, that the different tribes are represented here as bringing their offerings precisely in the same order in which they encamped about the tabernacle (see Nu ch. 2, 10), beginning at the East, then proceeding to the South, then to the West, and ending with the North, according to the course of the sun. Thus God evinces that he “is not the author of confusion, but of peace” (1 Co 14:33).`
