## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`high places. Bamoth baal, “the high places of Baal,” probably the same as Bamoth mentioned in Nu 21:19, 20; evidently not far from Baal-meon, in the mountains of Abarim: for the Israelites were now encamped in the plains of Moab, which these mountains overlook. Baal, which signifies a lord or governor, was a name common to many idols; and probably here was the same as Chemosh, the god of Moab. Nu 25:2, 3. Dt 12:2. 2 Ch 11:15. Je 48:35. utmost. Nu 23:13.`
