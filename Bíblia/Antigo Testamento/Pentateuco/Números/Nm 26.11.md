## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`died not. It seems to be intimated in Nu 16:27, 31-33, that the sons and the little ones of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram, were swallowed up; but the text here expressly affirms, that the children of Korah “died not”; and their descendants were famous even in David’s time. On a close inspection, however, of ver. 27, we shall find, that “the sons and the little ones” of Dathan and Abiram alone are mentioned. Nu 16:5. Ex 6:24. 1 Ch 6:22-28. Ps ch. 42, 44, 45, etc. Titles.`
