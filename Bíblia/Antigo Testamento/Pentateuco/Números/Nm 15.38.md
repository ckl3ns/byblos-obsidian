## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`fringes in the borders. The word tzitzith properly denotes an ornament resembling a flower. From ver. 39, we learn that these were emblematical of the commands of God. That there is any analogy between a fringe and a precept, it would be bold to assert; but when a thing is appointed to represent another, no matter how different, that first object becomes the legitimate representative or sign of the other. Dt 22:12. Mt 9:20. 23:5. Lk 8:44.`
