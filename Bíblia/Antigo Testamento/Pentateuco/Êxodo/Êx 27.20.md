`pure oil olive. Things bruised, crushed, or beaten symbolize the Lord’s sufferings. Is =53:5. He +=2:10. for the light. See on Ex 25:31-37. to cause the lamp. Josephus says, that the whole of the seven lamps burned all night; and that in the morning four were extinguished, and three burned the whole of the day. Such might have been the practice in his time; but it appears sufficiently evident from Ex 30:8, and 1 S 3:3, that they were extinguished in the morning. to burn. Heb. to ascend up. Ex 25:37.
