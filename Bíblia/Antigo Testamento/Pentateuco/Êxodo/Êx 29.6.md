## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`mitre. Mitznepheth, from tzanaph, to wrap around, evidently means that covering of the head so universal in eastern countries, which we call turban, which consists of a cap, and a sash of fine linen or silk wound round its bottom. See on Ex 28:36-39. Le 8:9.`
