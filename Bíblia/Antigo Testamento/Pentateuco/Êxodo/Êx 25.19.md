## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`of the. or, of the matter of the. The cherubim were thus looking toward each other, and toward the Mercy-seat, and therefore gazing on the blood. Habershon suggests “These last facts seem to show that the cherubim typify the redeemed; rent in the vail—crucified with Christ, Ga 2:20; one with Him, Jn 17:21, 23; fellowship with one another and with Himself, 1 J 1:3, 7; owing all to the blood, 1 J 1:7” (Outline Studies, p. 57).`
