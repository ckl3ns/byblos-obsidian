Je 34:8, 15, 17. Ezk 46:17. cinnamon. Pr 7:17. SS 4:14, only. sweet calamus. Young renders spice-cane. On cane, see Ge 41:5. SS 4:14. Is 43:24. Je 6:20. Ezk 27:19.`
