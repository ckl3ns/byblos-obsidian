## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`tabernacle. See on Ex 26:1n, 2, etc. 31:7-9. 33:7n. 36:8-34. taches. Keraism, from karas, to bend, so called from their curved form; hooks, clasps, or any thing used for the purpose of fastening: the word taches is formed by aphaeresis from the French attacher, to fasten. They were equivalent, perhaps, to our hooks-and-eyes.`
