## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`The First Commandment. Thou shalt have no other gods before me. shalt. lit. wilt. ƒ96C10, Jg +5:21. Heterosis of Tenses: The future for the imperative B523. The future of the Indicative is by Hebrew idiom frequently used for the Imperative. When this is the case, the Imperative is very forcible and emphatic; not being so much a mere command as the assertion of a fact which could hardly be otherwise.
