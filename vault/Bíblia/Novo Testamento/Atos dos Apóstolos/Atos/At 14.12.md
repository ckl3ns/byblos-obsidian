## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Jupiter. i.e. father who helps, ❅S#2203g. ver. 13. Jupiter was the supreme god of the heathen; and Mercury was considered the god of eloquence. The ancients represent Jupiter as an aged man, large, noble, and majestic; and Mercury, young, light, and active; and it is very probable that Barnabas was a large, noble, well-made man; and St. Paul, young active, and eloquent. ver. 13. Ac 19:35. 28:11. Mercurius. i.e. eloquent, learned, shrewd, crafty; reciprocal activity, ❅S#2060g. Ro 16:14, Hermes.`
