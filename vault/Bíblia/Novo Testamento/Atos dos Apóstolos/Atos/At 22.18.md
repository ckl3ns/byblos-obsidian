## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`saw. ver. 14. Make. Ac 9:29. Mt 10:14, 23. Lk 21:21. quickly out. The History in Acts ch. 9 might lead us to suppose that Paul’s stay in Jerusalem had been longer, but this vagueness is brought by the explanation here into harmony with Ga 1:18, where we are told the time was fifteen days. Such round-about consistency is by undesigned coincidence (see related notes listed at Ac 12:12). for. Ex 3:19. Ezk 3:6, 7. not receive. ƒ175B, Ge +21:16. Mt 10:14.`
