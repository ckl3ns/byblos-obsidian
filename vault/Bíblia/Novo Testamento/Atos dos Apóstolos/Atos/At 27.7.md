Ti 1:5, 12. under. ver. 4, 16. Crete. or, Candy. i.e. carnal; fleshly, ❅S#2914g. ver. 12, 13, 21. Ti 1:5. Compare ❅S#2912g: Ac 2:11, Cretes. Ti 1:12, Cretians. Crete, now Candy, is a large island in the Mediterranean, 250 miles in length, 50 in breadth, and 600 in circumference, lying at the entrance of the Aegean sea. Salmone. i.e. clothed; garment; peaceable; commotion; from the surging, ❅S#4534g. Salmone, now Salamina, was a city and cape on the east of the island of Crete.`
