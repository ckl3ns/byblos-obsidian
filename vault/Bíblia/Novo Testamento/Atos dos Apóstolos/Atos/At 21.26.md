Yet on several occasions he submitted to the law. He circumcised Timothy (Ac 16:3), and in this text purified himself in the temple; but in these cases, and always, in order to conciliate the Jews, he being unwilling to give unnecessary offense. Thus these instances in the History correspond with the doctrine in the Epistle, in a manner to constitute an undesigned coincidence (see related notes listed at Ac 12:12n). entered.
