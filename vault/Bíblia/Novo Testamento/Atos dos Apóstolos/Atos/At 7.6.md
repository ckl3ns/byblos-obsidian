## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`That. Ge ▶15:13, 16. sojourn. Ex 2:22. He 11:9. four. Stephen here uses the round number 400, leaving out the odd tens; for it is evident, from the parallel passages, as well as Josephus (Ant. l. ii. c. 1. sec. 9. Bel. l. v. c. 9. sec. 4) that the real number of years was 430. ver. 17. Ge 15:16. Ex *12:40, 41. Ro *4:18, 21. Ga 3:17.`
