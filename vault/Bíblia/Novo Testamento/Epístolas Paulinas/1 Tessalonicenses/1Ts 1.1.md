## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Paul. 2 Th 1:1, 2. Silvanus. Silas, or Silvanus, and Timothy, did not come to the Apostle, when driven from Thessalonica and Berea, till after his arrival at Athens, nor did they continue with him in that city, being sent speedily back to Thessalonica (1 Th 3:1. Ac 17:10-15); which shows that this epistle could not have been written from Athens, but from Corinth, where they afterwards rejoined him (Ac 18:1-6).
