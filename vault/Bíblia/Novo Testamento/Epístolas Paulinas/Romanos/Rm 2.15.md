Every man knows he ought to do certain things, and ought not do certain other things, evidenced every time he passes judgment on the actions of others (Ro 2:1). If only those who reject the gospel are lost, it would be safer not to preach the gospel. If a person could be saved without hearing the gospel, simply because he has not heard, then it would logically be safer not to preach the gospel, so that no one would hear, and all would be saved!
