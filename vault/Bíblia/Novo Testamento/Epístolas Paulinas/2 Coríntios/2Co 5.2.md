## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`we groan. ver. 4. Ro 7:24. *8:23. 1 P 1:6, 7. earnestly. Ph *1:23. clothed. ver. 3, 4. Lk +24:49. 1 Co *15:53, 54. from heaven. Thus the change to a resurrected, glorified body occurs on earth (Peters, Theocratic Kingdom, vol. 2, p. 241). The more usual interpretation of this passage, that it teaches we receive temporary bodies in heaven immediately after death, is faulty, because it makes the future resurrection of the body unnecessary and redundant.`
