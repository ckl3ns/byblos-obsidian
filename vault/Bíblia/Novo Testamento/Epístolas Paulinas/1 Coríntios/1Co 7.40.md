## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`she. ver. 1, 8, 26, 35. if she. ƒ184C, Mt +4:9. my judgment. ver. +6. I think. Rather, “I judge (or consider) also that I have the Spirit of God;” for dokein is frequently used to express not what is doubtful, but what is true and certain. “Think” implies the certainty of assured belief (1 Co 10:12. Lk 1:3. 10:36. 12:40. 17:9. 22:24. Jn ✓5:39. Ac 15:25, 28, 34. 26:9). ver. 25. 1 Co 4:9. 9:1-3. *14:36, 37. 2 Co 10:8-10. 12:11. 1 Th 4:8. 2 P 3:15, 16. Spirit. Gr. pneuma, ƒ121A1, Lk +1:17n. “Spirit of God” (pneuma theou), i.e. Divine spirit, Divine power, Divine inspiration. It refers to the gift of inspiration which he had, and not the giver (E. W. Bullinger, The Giver and His Gifts, p. 125).`
