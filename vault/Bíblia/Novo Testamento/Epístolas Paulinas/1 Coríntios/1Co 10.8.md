## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Neither let us. 1 Co +*6:9, 18. Nu *25:1, 5, 9. Ps 106:29. Ac +15:20. Re 2:14. as some. Nu 25:1. 31:16. and fell. Nu *25:9. Ps 106:29. three and twenty thousand. Both the LXX. and Hebrew texts have twenty-four thousand, but as both Moses and Paul were using round numbers, without intending to be mathematically exact in common speech, any number between the two amounts may be stated roundly as either the one or the other, as Hodge explains.`
