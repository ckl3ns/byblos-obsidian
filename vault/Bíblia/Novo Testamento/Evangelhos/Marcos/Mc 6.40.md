## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`in ranks. ƒ108E4, ver. 7. by hundreds. It is generally supposed that they were so arranged as to be a hundred in rank, or depth, and fifty in front, or file; which would make the number just five thousand, and will reconcile this account with St. Luke’s, who only speaks of their sitting down by fifties. Lk 9:14, 15.`
