(1) A woman who followed Jesus from Galilee and witnessed his crucifixion “afar off,” Mk 15:40; 16:1. She was the wife of Zebedee and the mother of the apostles James and John. (2) “The daughter of Herodias.” She danced before Herod, and asked for the head of John the Baptist, Mt 14:6-8; Mk 6:22-25. She is not named in the New Testament, but is mentioned by Josephus, an ancient Jewish historian.`
