## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`very early. Mr. West supposes that the women made two different visits to the sepulchre, and, in consequence of that, two distinct reports to the disciples; that Mary Magdalene, with the other Mary and Salome, set out not only early, but very early in the morning, lian proi, i.e. before the time appointed to meet Joanna and the other women there (Lk 24:10). This interpretation, which is adopted by several eminent writers, is very probable, and reconciles the apparent discrepancy in the evangelists. Mt 28:1. Lk *24:1. Jn *20:1. the first day. ver. 9. came. ƒ171J14, Jon +1:3. i.e. went.`
