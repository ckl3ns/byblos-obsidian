Called Lebbaeus in Mt 10:3, Thaddaeus in Mk 3:18, and Jude in Ju 1. (5) Judas of Galilee, the leader of an insurrection against the Roman enrolment under Augustus, successful at first, but finally defeated, Ac 5:37. (6) Judas, in whose house Paul found shelter in Damascus during his blindness, Ac 9:11-17. (7) Judas, surnamed Barsabas, who, together with Paul, Barnabas, and Silas, was chosen to carry the decisions of the Council of Jerusalem, to Antioch, Ac 15:22-33. the brother. Mt 10:3, Lebbeus, Thaddeus. Mk 3:18, Thaddeus. Jn 14:22. Ju *1. Judas Iscariot. Mt 26:14-16. 27:3-5. Jn *6:70, 71. Ac 1:16-20, 25. the traitor. Ps 41:9. 1 J *2:19.`
