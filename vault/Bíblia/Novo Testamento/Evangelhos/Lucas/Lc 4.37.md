## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the fame. Eekos, the “sound;” a very elegant metaphor, says Dr. Adam Clarke. The people are represented as struck with astonishment, and the sound goes out through all the coasts; in allusion to the propagation of sound by a smart stroke upon any substance. ver. 14. Is 52:13. Mt 4:23-25. 9:26. Mk 1:28, 45. 6:14.`
