## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`whither they went. ver. 13. he made. That is, he was directing his steps as if to go onwards; and so he doubtless would, had he not been withheld by their friendly importunities. There is not the smallest ground for founding a charge of dissimulation against our Savior, or affording any encouragement to dissimulation in others. Ge 19:2. 32:26. 42:7. 1 S ◐*16:2n. Mk 6:48.`
