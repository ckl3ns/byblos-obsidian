## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`wrote. Mt 27:37. Mk 15:26. Lk 23:38. And the. The apparent discrepancy between the accounts of this title given by the Evangelists, which has been urged as an objection against their inspiration and veracity, has been satisfactorily accounted for by Dr. Townson; who supposes that, as it was written in Hebrew, Greek, and Latin, it might have slightly varied in each language; and that, as St. Luke and St. John wrote for the Gentiles, they would prefer the Greek inscription, that St. Matthew, addressing the Jews, would use the Hebrew, and that St. Mark, writing to the Romans, would naturally give the Latin. JESUS. ver. 3, 12. Jn 1:45, 46, 49. 18:33. Ac 3:6. 26:9. OF NAZARETH. Jn 18:5. THE KING. ver. +3.`
