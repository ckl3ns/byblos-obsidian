## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`from Galilee. Mt 2:22. Mk *1:9. Lk *3:21. to Jordan. lit. upon (Gr. epi) Jordan. Dale notes that this construction places Jesus upon the bank of the Jordan where he was baptized (Christic Baptism, p. 145). See related note on the preposition epi at Ac 2:38. Compare notes on Mk 1:4, 5, 9. baptized. T#35. He 7:11-14.`
