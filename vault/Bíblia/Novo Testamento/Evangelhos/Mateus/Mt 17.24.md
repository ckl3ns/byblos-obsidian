## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`when. Mk 9:33. Capernaum. Mt 4:13. tribute. “Gr. didrachma, in value fifteen pence.” Ge 24:22. Ex 30:13. 38:26. 2 K 12:4. 2 Ch 24:6. Ne *10:32, 33. This tribute seems to have been the half shekel which every male among the Jews paid yearly for the support of the temple, and which was continued by them, wherever dispersed, till after the time of Vespasian (See Josephus, Bel. l. vii. c. 6). Doth not. Mt +*5:17. master. Mt 22:24. pay. Jn +4:8n.`
