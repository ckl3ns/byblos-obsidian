## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hariph. i.e. plucking off; autumnal; winter; autumn rains; reproach, ❅S#2756h. Ne 7:24. 10:19. (1) A Jew some of whose posterity went up from Babylon, Ne 7:24. (2) The head of a family of Jews, who sealed the covenant made by Nehemiah, Ne 10:19. Hariph and Jorah were probably two distinct names of this person: Ezr 2:18, Jorah. 2 Ch 34:20n.`
