## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the fountain gate. Ne 2:14. 3:15, etc. the stairs. Jerusalem was built on very uneven ground, some hills being enclosed within the walls; there was a necessity, therefore, for steps, by which to ascend and descend; probably similar to what is seen in the city of Bristol. Ne 3:15. 2 S 5:7-9. water gate. Ne 3:26. 8:1, 3, 16.`
