## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Tel-haresha. i.e. hill of the magus; heap of the artificer; height of an enchanter; hill of plowing,❅S#8521h. Ne 7:61. Ezr 2:59. The first of these variations only exists in the translation; the original being uniformly “Tel-harsha:” the latter simply arises from the insertion of a wav; being written Addan, in the parallel passage, and “Addon” here. Ezr 2:59, Tel-harsa, Addan. Addon. i.e. foundation; depression; calamity, ❅S#114h. seed. or, pedigree.`
