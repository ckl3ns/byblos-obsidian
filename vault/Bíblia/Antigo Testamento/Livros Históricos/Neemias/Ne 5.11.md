## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Restore. Ex 22:1. Le 6:4, 5. Nu 5:7. 1 S 12:3. 2 S 12:6. Is 58:6. Lk 3:8. *19:8. their lands. ver. 3, 4. the hundredth. This was probably the rate of interest which they obliged their poor debtors to pay each month, which would amount to about 12◐. Another author (Volney, Travels, vol. ii. p. 410) states that this is the lowest rate of interest in Syria: the usual rate is 20◐; and it is sometimes as high as 30◐. Is +24:2.`
