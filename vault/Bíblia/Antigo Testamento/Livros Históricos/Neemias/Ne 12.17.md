## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Miniamin. i.e. from or on the right hand,❅S#4509h. Ne 12:17, 41. 2 Ch 31:15. (1) One of those in charge of receiving and distributing the freewill offerings in the temple in the time of Hezekiah, 2 Ch 31:15. (2) A father’s house in the days of the high priest Joiakim, Ne 12:17. (3) A priest who blew a trumpet at the dedication of the wall of Jerusalem, Ne 12:41. The LXX. and Vulgate have here Miamin. ver. 5, Miamin. Moadiah. i.e. set time of Jah; assembly or festival of Jah, ❅S#4153h. ver. 5, Maadiah. Piltai. i.e. flight; deliverance of the Lord; my escapes, ❅S#6408h.`
