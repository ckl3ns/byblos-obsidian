## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Amon. i.e. architect; workman; artificer; to nourish; a multitude; to be faithful, ❅S#526h. Ne 7:59. 1 K 22:26. 2 K 21:18, 19, 23, 24, 25. 1 Ch 3:14. 2 Ch 18:25. 33:20, 21, 22, 23, 25. Je 1:2. 25:3. (1) A son of Manasseh, king of Judah, 644-642 B.C. 2 K 21:18-26; 2 Ch 33:20. (2) Governor of Ramoth Gilead, 1 K 22:26. (3) A servant of Solomon, Ezr 2:57; Ne 7:59. Instead of Amon, two of De Rossi’s MSS. have Amin, and the LXX. have “Emim;” which nearly approaches the Ami of Ezra. Ezr 2:57, Ami.`
