## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Binnui. Ne 10:9. another piece. That which had been left by Azariah, after he had repaired the wall by his own house. It is probable that some of the principal people were either obliged, or voluntarily offered, to repair those parts of the wall which were opposite, or adjacent, to their own houses. The names of those who repaired the walls are commemorated, because it was an undertaking of piety, virtue, and courage, to restore the holy city. ver. 11, 19, 27. the turning. ver. 20.`
