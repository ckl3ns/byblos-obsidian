## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Nehemiah. i.e. whom Jehovah comforts; comforted of Jah; comfort of the Lord, ❅S#5166h. Ne 1:1. 3:16. 7:7. 8:9. *10:1h, 8. 12:26, 47. Ezr 2:2. (1) Son of Hachaliah, the governor of Judea, in the reign of Artaxerxes Longimanus, Ne 1:1; 8:9; 10:1. (2) One who helped to build the wall, Ne 3:16. (3) One who came up with Zerubbabel, Ezr 2:2; Ne 7:7. Hachaliah. i.e. reddened or dimmed by Jah; whose eyes Jehovah enlivens; dark flashing of the Lord; the waiting on Jah, ❅S#2446h. Ne 1:1. 10:1. in the month. Ezr 10:9. Zc 7:1. in the twentieth. Ezr 7:7. Shushan. Shushan, or Susa, was the capital of Susiana, a province of Persia, and the winter residence of the Persian monarchs; situated about 252 miles east of Babylon, and the same distance south south-east of Ecbatana, in lat. 32 degrees, long. 49 degrees. The circumference of its walls was above 120 stadia. Shouster is supposed to occupy its site. Est 1:2. 3:15. Da 8:2.`
