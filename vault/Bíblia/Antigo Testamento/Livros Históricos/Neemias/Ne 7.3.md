## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Let not the gates. That is, the gates were not to be opened till sunrise, and to be shut at sunset; which is still the custom in many cities of the East. If a traveler arrives after sunset, he finds the gates shut, and on no consideration will they open them till the morning. Ne 13:19. Ps 127:1. Mt 10:16. every one to be. Ne 3:23, 28-30.`
