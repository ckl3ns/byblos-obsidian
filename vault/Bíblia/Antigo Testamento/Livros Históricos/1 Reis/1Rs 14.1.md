`Abijah. i.e. my father is Jah, ✣S#29h. (1) A son of Jeroboam who died young, 1 K 14:1. (2) A priest in the time of David who was at the head of the eighth course in the temple service, 1 Ch 24:10; Lk 1:5, Abiah. (3) One of the sons of Rehoboam, the son of Solomon, by Maachah, the daughter of Absalom: 2 Ch 11:20, 22. 12:16. 13:1, 2, 3, 4, 15, 17, 19, 20, 21, 22. 14:1. (4) The mother of Hezekiah, king of Judah, 2 Ch 29:1. (5) A priest that sealed the covenant made by Nehemiah and the people to serve the Lord, Ne 10:7. (6) A priest that went up from Babylon with Zerubbabel, Ne 12:4, 17. May be the same as number 5. that time. 1 K 13:33, 34. the son. ver. 12, 13. Ex 20:5. 1 S 4:19, 20. 31:2. 2 S 12:15.`
