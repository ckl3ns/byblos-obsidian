## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Take. 2 S 20:6. and cause. Maimonides informs us, that it was a capital offence for any one to ride on the king’s mule, to sit on his throne, or to handle his sceptre, without permission; and as David ordered Solomon to ride on his own mule, etc., it was ample evidence that he had appointed him his successor. to ride. ver. 5, 38, 44. Ge 41:43. Est 6:6-11. mine own mule. Heb. the mule which belongeth to me. Le 19:19. Gihon. ver. 38, 45. 2 Ch 32:30. 33:14.`
