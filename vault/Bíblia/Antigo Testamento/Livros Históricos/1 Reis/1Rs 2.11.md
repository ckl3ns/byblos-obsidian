## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`reigned over. 2 S 5:4. 1 Ch 29:26, 27. seven years. ƒ171T7. Synecdoche of the Part B656. In chronology a part of a time or period is sometimes put for the whole of such period. Here, “seven years” is put for seven and a half years. Compare 2 S 2:11. For another instance of this figure see 2 K 24:8.`
