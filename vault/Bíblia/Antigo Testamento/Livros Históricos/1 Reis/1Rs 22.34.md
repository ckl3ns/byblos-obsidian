`at a venture. Heb. in his simplicity. The marginal rendering is more accurate, and, as Carr observes, “only implies the unconsciousness of the agent in carrying out the divine purpose, and certainly conveys no thought of chance, for never was weapon more divinely guided in its aim” (Horae Biblicae, p. 37).
