(2) An Israelite whose son, Baasha, conspired against Nadab, the son of Jeroboam, and reigned in his stead: 1 K 15:27, 33. 21:22. 2 K 9:9. (3) Son of Jerahmeel, 1 Ch 2:25. (4) One of David’s mighty men, 1 Ch 11:36. (5) A Levite set over the treasures of God’s house in David’s time, 1 Ch 26:20.
