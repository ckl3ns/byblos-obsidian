## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the eyes. 2 Ch 20:12. Ps 25:15. 123:1, 2. Zc 3:9. that thou. At this time the monarchy of Israel was unsettled: no man knew who was to succeed to the crown; and the minds of the people were as unsettled as the succession. It was neither hereditary nor elective: the king, as was anciently the case in most countries, named his successor; but in this instance, God had already assigned the throne to Solomon. 2 S 23:2. 1 Ch 22:8-10. 28:5, 6, 10. 29:1.`
