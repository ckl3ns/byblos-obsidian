## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Let not him, etc. ƒ138C, Ge +22:14. This was no doubt a proverbial mode of expression. Jonathan renders it: “Let not him who girds himself, and goes down to the battle, boast as he who has conquered and returned from it.” 1 S 14:6, 12, 13. 17:44-47. Pr *27:1. Ec 9:11. Is 10:15, 16. Mt 26:33-35, 75. harness. The word harness is an obsolete word for armor, derived from the French harnois; see Ex 13:18.`
