## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a widow’s son. Heb. the son of a widow woman. tribe. The mother of Hiram (not the Tyrian king mentioned before, but an intelligent coppersmith, of Jewish extraction by his mother’s side) in Chronicles is said to have been of “the daughters of Dan;” and she might have been of Naphtali by her father, and of Dan by her mother; or she might originally be of the tribe of Dan, and have been first married to a man of the tribe of Naphtali; and, in either case, she might be indifferently called “of the tribe of Naphtali,” or of “the daughters of Dan.” Naphtali. 2 Ch 2:14. his father. 2 Ch 4:16. worker. or, plower, graver. 1 K 19:19. Jb 1:14. in brass. Ezk 27:13. he was filled. Ex 31:2-6. 35:30-35. 36:1, 2, 8. 2 Ch 2:13, 14. 4:11. Is 28:26. Da 1:17.`
