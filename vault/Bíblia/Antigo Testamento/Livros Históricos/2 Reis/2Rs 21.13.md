## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`I will stretch. This metaphor is taken from the custom of using a line in measuring land, and in dividing portions of it among several persons. Samaria was taken, pillaged, and ruined, and its inhabitants carried into captivity: Jerusalem shall have the same measure. 2 K 17:6. Is 10:22. 28:17. 34:11. La 2:8. Ezk 23:31-34. Am 7:7, 8. Zc 1:16. the plummet. 2 K 10:11. 1 K 21:21-24. I will wipe. ƒ147A, Ge +50:24. I will empty Jerusalem of all its wealth and inhabitants, as truly as a dish turned up and wiped is emptied of its contents. 1 K 14:10. Is 14:23. Je 25:9. Ezk 24:10, 11. Re 18:21-23. wiping it, and turning it upside down. Heb. he wipeth and turneth it upon the face thereof.`
