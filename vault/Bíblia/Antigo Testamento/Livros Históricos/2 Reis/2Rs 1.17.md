## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Jehoram. i.e. the Lord exalts, ❅S#3088h. (1) The eldest son of Jehoshaphat, and his successor as king of Judah, 1 K 22:50. 2 K 1:17. 8:16, 25, 29. 12:18. 2 Ch 21:1, 3, 4, 5, 9, 16. 22:1, 6, 11. (2) A son of Ahab, king of Israel, who succeeded to the throne on the death of Ahaziah, who had no son. 2 K 1:17. 3:1, 6. 9:24. 2 Ch 22:5, 6, 7. (3) A priest employed by Jehoshaphat to instruct the people, 2 Ch 17:8. As it is said in 2 K 3:1, that he began his reign in the eighteenth of Jehoshaphat, it is supposed that Jehoshaphat admitted his son Jehoram to reign with him eight or nine years before his death. “The second year that Jehoram was Prorex, and the eighteenth of Jehoshaphat.” in the second. 2 K 3:1. 8:16, 17. 1 K 22:51.`
