Tartak. i.e. intense darkness; a flat basket, ❅S#8662h, only here. Sepharvites. i.e. inhabitants of Sepharvaim, ❅S#5616h, only here. burnt their children. See on ver. 17. Le *18:21. Dt *12:30, 31. Adrammelech. i.e. honor of the king. 2 K 19:37. Anammelech. i.e. the affliction of the king, ❅S#6048h.`
