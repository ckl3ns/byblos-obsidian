## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`stood by. The Orientals considered a seat by a pillar or column as particularly honorable. Thus Homer (Od. xxiii.) places Ulysses on a lofty throne by a pillar; and in the eighth book of the Odyssey he twice alludes to the same custom.
