## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the young man. Hannaar hannavee, not as some would render, “the servant of the prophet,” but correctly rendered by our venerable translators, “the young man, the prophet;” for hannaar, “the young man,” is not in regimine, but in appositione, with hanavee, “the prophet.” Is +40:13mg. the prophet. 2 K 2:3.`
