## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`from his brethren. Heb. from the face of. ƒ144A1, Ge +11:8. Tob. i.e. good; goodness, ❅S#2897h. ver. 5. Probably the same as Ish-tob; and appears to have been a part of Syria, near Zobah, Rehob, and Maachah, east of Jordan, and in the most northern part of the portion of Manasseh. If so, it could not be far from Gilead, the country of Jephthah. This country is called Tobie or Tubin, 1 Mac 5:13; and the Jews who inhabited this district, Tubieni, 2 Mac 12:17. 2 S 10:6, 8. vain men. Jg 9:4. 1 S 22:2. 27:2. 30:22-24. Jb 30:1-10. Ac 17:5.`
