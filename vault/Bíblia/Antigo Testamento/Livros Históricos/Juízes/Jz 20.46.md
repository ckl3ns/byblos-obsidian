## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`twenty and five thousand. ver. 15, 35. Besides the odd hundred expressed (ver. 35); but here only the great number is expressed, the less being omitted, as inconsiderable; which way of numbering is frequent in Scripture, as Jg 11:26; 2 S 5:5, and in other authors, and in vulgar use; as when they are called seventy interpreters, who in truth and exactness were seventy-two. Here are also a thousand more omitted, because here he speaks only of them who fell in that third day of battle (Matthew Poole).`
