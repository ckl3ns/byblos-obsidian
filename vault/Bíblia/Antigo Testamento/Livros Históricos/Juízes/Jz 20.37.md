## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the liers in wait hasted. Jsh 8:15, 19. drew themselves along. or, made a long sound with the trumpets. Ex 19:13. Jsh 6:5. or, marched or went. Heb. drew their feet. So this verb is often used, as Ge 37:28. Ex 12:21. Jg 4:6. Jb 21:33. Here, a reference to marching in rank and file, as armies do (Matthew Poole).`
