## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`I am now. The LXX. read, “I am going to my own house;” which is probably the true reading, as we find (ver. 29) that he really went home; yet he might have gone previously to Shiloh, or to “the house of the Lord,” because that was also in mount Ephraim. the house.
