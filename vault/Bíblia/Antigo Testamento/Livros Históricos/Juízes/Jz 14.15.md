## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`on the seventh day. The LXX. reads “on the fourth day;” with which the Syriac and Arabic agree. This, as Dr. Wall observes, is certainly right; for it appears from ver. 17, that she wept the remainder of the seven days; for which there could have been no time, if they did not threaten her till the seventh. Entice. Jg 16:5. Ge 3:1-6. Pr 1:11. 5:3. 6:26. Mi 7:5. lest we burn. Jg 12:1. 15:6. take that we have. Heb. possess us, or, impoverish us. Dt 28:42mg.`
