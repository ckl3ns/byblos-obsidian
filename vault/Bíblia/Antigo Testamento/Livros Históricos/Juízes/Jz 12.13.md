## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2840. B.C. 1164. An. Ex. Is. 327. Abdon. i.e. servile; service; servitude, ❅S#5658h. ver. 15. 1 Ch 8:23, 30. 9:36. 2 Ch 34:20. “A civil judge also in northeast Israel.” (1) A city of Asher which was afterwards assigned to the Levites, Jsh 21:30; 1 Ch 6:74. (2) A man of Ephraim who was one of the judges of Israel, Jg 12:13, 15. (3) A descendant of Benjamin, 1 Ch 8:23. (4) The eldest son of the father of Gibeon, 1 Ch 8:30; 9:36. (5) The name apparently of one of the priests in the time of Josiah, king of Judah, whom the king sent to enquire of the Lord when the book of the law had been found, 2 Ch 34:20. Hillel. i.e. praising, ❅S#1985h. ver. 15.`
