## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`woman. ver. 15, 20. 2 S 11:21. 20:21. Jb 31:3. Je 49:20. 50:45. and all to. An antiquated expression, meaning “full intention” to complete an object. “All to,” observes Dr. Johnson, “is a particle of mere enforcement.” The original is wattaritz eth gulgalto, which is simply, as the LXX. render, kai eklase to kranion auton, “and she brake his skull.” Plutarch relates, that Pyrrhus was killed at the siege of Thebes, by a piece of a tile, which a woman threw upon his head. brake. S#7533h. Jg 10:8mg. Dt +28:33.`
