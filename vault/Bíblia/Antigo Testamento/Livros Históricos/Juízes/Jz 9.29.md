## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`would to God. The very words and conduct of a sly, hypocritical demagogue. 2 S 15:4. 1 K 20:11. Ps 10:3. Ro 1:30, 31. And he said. Rather, “and I would say to Abimelech,” as the LXX. renders; for, as Dr. Wall observes, this was probably not said in the presence of Abimelech; but at an intemperate feast, in his absence, when he boasted he would challenge him. Increase thine army. 2 S 2:14-17. 2 K 14:8. 18:23. Is 36:8, 9.`
