## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`privily. Heb. craftily, or, to Tormah. Ps 119:118. Tormah is supposed to be the same place as Arumah in ver. 41. they fortify. Under pretense of repairing the walls and towers, they were actually putting the place in a state of defence, intending to seize on the government as soon as they found Abimelech coming against them.`
