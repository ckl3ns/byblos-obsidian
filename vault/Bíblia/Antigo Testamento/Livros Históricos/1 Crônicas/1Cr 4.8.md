## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Coz. i.e. thorn; trouble, ❅S#6976h. 1 Ch 4:8. 24:+10, Hakoz. Ezr 2:+61, Koz. Ne 3:4, 21. 7:63. (1) Father of Anub and Zobebah, 1 Ch 4:8. (2) The son of Aaron, to whom the seventh lot fell, 1 Ch 24:10. (3) One whose children returned from Babylon, Ne 7:63. (4) One whose son helped build the wall, Ne 3:4, 21. Anub. i.e. clustered, ❅S#6036h. Zobebah. i.e. sluggish; an army, ❅S#6637h. Aharhel. i.e. behind the breastwork, ❅S#316h. Harum. i.e. high, ❅S#2037h. Probably Jabez should be mentioned here; as otherwise he is as a consequent without an antecedent.`
