## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`they went. This expedition of the Simeonites took place in the days of Hezekiah, and, as Calmet conjectures, near the time of the captivity of the ten tribes, when the remnant of Simeon would feel themselves obliged to retire more southward into Arabia Petraea, for fear of the Jews, and to seek pasture for their flocks. Gedor. ver. 4, 18. Jsh 12:13, Geder. 15:58.`
