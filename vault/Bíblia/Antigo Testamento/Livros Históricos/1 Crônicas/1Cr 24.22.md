## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Izharites. i.e. descendants of Izhar (i.e. anointed; oil, Ex +6:18), ❅S#3325h. 1 Ch 24:22. 26:23, 29. Nu 3:27. The original is uniformly Izharites. 1 Ch 23:18. Ex 6:21. Nu 3:19, 27, Izeharites. Shelomoth. i.e. peaceful; pacifications; retributions, ❅S#8013h. 1 Ch 23:9. 24:22, 22. 26:25, 26. The variation of “Shelomith” and “Shelomoth” arises from the mutation of wav and yood. 1 Ch 23:18. 26:26, Shelomith. Jahath. i.e. he affrights. 1 Ch +4:2.`
