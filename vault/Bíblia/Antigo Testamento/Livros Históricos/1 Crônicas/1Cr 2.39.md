## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Helez. i.e. strength, ❅S#2503h. 2 S 23:26. (1) One of David’s valiant men, 2 S 23:26; 1 Ch 11:27. (2) A descendant of Jerahmeel, 1 Ch 2:39. Eleasah. i.e. God has wrought, ❅S#501h. 1 Ch 2:39, 40. 8:37. 9:43. Ezr 10:22. Je 29:3. (1) Descended from Sheshan’s daughter, 1 Ch 2:39. (2) One of the stock of Saul, 1 Ch 8:37; 9:43. (3) Rendered “Elasah,” the bearer of Jeremiah’s letter to the captives in Babylon, Je 29:3.`
