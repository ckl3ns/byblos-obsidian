## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hilkiah. i.e. portion of Jah. 2 K +18:18. Tebaliah. i.e. goodness of God; dipped of Jehovah; whom Jehovah has purified, ❅S#2882h. Strong in the lexicon to his exhaustive concordance gives the definition for this name: “Jah has dipped.” John Ritchie in his Scripture Proper Names gives “Immersed of God, (purified).” The Hebrew word tabal (❅S#2881h, Ex 12:22) favors the definition “dip,” often merely meaning to “moisten.” Zechariah. i.e. remembered of Jah. Zc +1:1.`
