## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Assir. Ex +6:24. As Salathiel was not the son of Assir, but of Jeconiah, it is probable that the word assir, which signifies a prisoner, is an epithet applied to Jeconiah, who was a long time a prisoner at Babylon.
