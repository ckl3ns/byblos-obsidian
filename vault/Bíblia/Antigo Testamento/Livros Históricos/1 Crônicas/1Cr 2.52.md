`Shobal. Ge +36:20. Haroeh. i.e. vision; prophet, ❅S#7204h. or, Reaiah, 1 Ch 4:2. As Haroeh and Reaiah have nearly the same signification, it is probable they were deemed perfectly interchangeable, and indifferently applied. half of the Manahethites. i.e. midst of the resting places, ❅S#2679h. or, half of the Menuchites, or, Hatsiham-menuchoth.`
