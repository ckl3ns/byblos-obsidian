Dalaiah. i.e. drawn of Jah, delivered, ❅S#1806h. 1 Ch 3:24. Ezr 2:60. Ne 6:10. 7:62. Also S#1806h, 1 Ch 24:18. Je 36:12, 25. Usually rendered “Delaiah,” Je +36:12.
