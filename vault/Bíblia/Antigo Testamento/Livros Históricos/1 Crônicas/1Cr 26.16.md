That is, ejection; probably the gate through which all the filth, which from time to time might accumulate in the temple and its courts, was cast out. causeway. Nu 20:19. Jg 5:20mg. 1 K 10:5. 2 Ch 9:4. going up. 1 K 10:5. 2 Ch 9:4. Ne 3:31. ward against ward. That is, their stations were opposite to each other; as the north to the south, and the east to the west. ver. 12. 1 Ch 25:8. Ne 12:24.`
