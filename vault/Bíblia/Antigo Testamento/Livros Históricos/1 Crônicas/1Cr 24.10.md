## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hakkoz. i.e. the thorn, ❅S#6976h. Abijah. i.e. my father is Jah. 1 K +14:1. As the Evangelist Luke mentions the course of Abia, it is evident that these courses of the priests, established by David, no doubt under Divine direction, were continued, with some alteration, till the days of Christ: these records must therefore have been very useful after the Babylonian captivity. Ne 12:4, 17. Lk 1:5, Abia.`
