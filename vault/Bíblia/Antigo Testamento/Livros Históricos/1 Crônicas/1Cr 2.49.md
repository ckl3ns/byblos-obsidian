## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the father of Madmannah. See on ver. 42. Madmannah was a city situated in the southern part of Judah, and towards Gaza, according to Eusebius. Jsh +15:31. Is 10:31, Madmenah. Sheva. 2 S +20:25. Macbenah. i.e. poverty of the son, ❅S#4343h. Gibea. i.e. a hill, ❅S#1388h. It is probable that this was not Gibeah of Benjamin, and the royal residence of Saul, but Gibeah in the tribe of Judah, to which tribe all these other cities belonged. Jsh 15:57. 2 S 21:6, Gibeah. Achsa. i.e. anklet, ❅S#5915h. Not as Jsh 15:16, 17. Jg 1:12, 13. 1 Ch 2:49.`
