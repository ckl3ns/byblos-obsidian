## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the king. From ver. 23, we learn that the king of Hebron was one of the five whom Joshua slew and hanged on five trees at Makkedah. This slaying of the king of Hebron, therefore, must either refer to what had already been done, or the Hebronites had set up another king, whom Joshua now slew when he took the city. souls. Heb. nephesh, ver. +*28. according. ver. 35. utterly. Jsh +8:26. souls. i.e. nephesh, ver. +*28.`
