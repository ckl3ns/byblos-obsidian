## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Sharaim. i.e. two gates, 1 S 17:52. 1 Ch 4:31. Adithaim. i.e. two ornaments; double prey, ❅S#5723h. Eusebius mentions two cities called Adatha, one towards Gaza, and the other east of Lydda. Gederah. i.e. the fence, ❅S#1449h. Jsh 12:13. Perhaps the same as the next mentioned place. and. or, or. Gederothaim. i.e. two fences; two sheepfolds, ❅S#1453h. fourteen cities. There are fifteen in all; but the two last seem to be only two names of the same city.`
