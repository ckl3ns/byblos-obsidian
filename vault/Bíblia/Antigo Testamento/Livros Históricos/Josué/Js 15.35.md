## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Jarmuth. i.e. a high place. Jsh 10:3, 23. 12:11. Ne 11:29. Adullam. i.e. a testimony to them; justice of the people; retreat; hiding place; struck with terror; their ornament. Jsh 12:15. 1 S 22:1. 2 Ch 11:7. Ne 11:30. Mi 1:15. Socoh. i.e. bough, ❅S#7755h. ver. 48. 1 S 17:1, 1. 1 K 4:10. 1 Ch 4:18. 2 Ch 11:7. 28:18. Eusebius says, there were two cities of Socoh, an upper and lower, nine miles from Eleutheropolis, towards Jerusalem. ver. 48. 1 S 17:1. 1 Ch 4:18. Azekah. i.e. fence, signet. Eusebius and Jerome say there was a town of this name in their time, between Jerusalem and Eleutheropolis. Jsh 10:10. 1 S 17:1.`
