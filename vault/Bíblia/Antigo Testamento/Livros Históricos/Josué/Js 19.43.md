## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

Ekron is placed by Eusebius between Ashdod and Jamnia, eastward; and probably the ruined village of Tookrair, mentioned by Dr. Richardson, situated on the top of a hill, and which he says seems to have been a place of considerable consequence, occupies its site. Jsh 15:45. Jg 1:18. 1 S 5:10. 7:14. Am 1:8. Zc 9:7.`
