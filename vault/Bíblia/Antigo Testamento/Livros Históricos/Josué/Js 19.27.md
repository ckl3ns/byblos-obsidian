Neiel. i.e. motion of God; we shall be shaken of God; the moving of God, ❅S#5272h. Cabul. i.e. sandy; a border, ❅S#3521h. (2) The name given by Hiram to the land containing twenty cities of Galilee which Solomon gave him, 1 K 9:13.`
