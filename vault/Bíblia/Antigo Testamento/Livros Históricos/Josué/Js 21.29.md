## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Jarmuth. i.e. height; elevated, high; he will be lifted up; casting down death, ❅S#3412h. Jsh 10:3, 5, 23. 12:11. 15:35. Ne 11:29. This seems to be the same city with Remeth, Jsh 19:19, 21, and Ramoth, 1 Ch 6:73, mentioned with Engannim. Jsh 10:3, 23. 12:11. (1) A town of Judah, Jsh 15:35; Ne 11:29. (2) A Levitical city of Issachar, Jsh 21:29, called Remeth in Jsh 19:21, and Ramoth in 1 Ch 6:73. En-gannim. Jsh 19:21. 1 Ch 6:73, Anem. Abdon. i.e. rigorous slavery; servitude. Perhaps the same as Hebron in Jsh 19:28. Helkath. Jsh 19:25, 34, Hukkok. 1 Ch 6:75, Hukok.`
