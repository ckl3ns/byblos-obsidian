## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Gibeon. Jsh 9:17. +*10:2. 1 K 3:4, 5. 9:2. Is 28:21. Ramah. i.e. the lofty place; the height. Situated, according to Eusebius, six miles from Jerusalem towards Bethel; though Jerome places it near Gaba, seven miles from Jerusalem. 1 S 1:1, Ramathaimzophim. 7:17. 15:34. Je *31:15. Ho 5:8. Mt 2:17, *18. 27:57, Arimathea. Beeroth. i.e. wells. Jsh 9:17. Dt 10:6. 2 S 4:2. Ezr 2:25. Ne 7:29. Eusebius says Beeroth was seven miles from Jerusalem, towards Nicopolis or Emmaus. Jerome, however, reads Neapolis or Shechem; but Reland prefers the former.`
