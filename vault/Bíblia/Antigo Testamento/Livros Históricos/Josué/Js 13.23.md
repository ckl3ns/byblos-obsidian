## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`and the border thereof. i.e. those cities or places which bordered upon Jordan. ƒ121L10, Metonymy of the Subject: the object is put for that which pertains or relates to it; here, “border” is put for the cities which are upon the border.`
