## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Chisloth-tabor. i.e. confidence of Tabor (i.e. choice, purity),” ❅S#3696h. Called Chasalus by Eusebius and Jerome, and placed at the foot of mount Tabor, eight miles east of Diocaesarea. ver. 22. Jg 4:6, 12. Ps 89:12. Daberath. i.e. led, submissive, obedient, ✣S#1705h. Jsh ◐21:28, Dabareh. 1 Ch *6:72. Josephus, who calls this town Dabaritta, or Darabitta, places it in the plain of Jezreel, or Esdraelon, on the confines of Samaria and Galilee. It is probably the Dabira which Jerome places towards mount Tabor, in the district of Diocaesarea; and the Debora or Daboura mentioned by travellers as a village at the foot of mount Tabor. Japhia. i.e. beautiful. Jsh 10:3. Probably Japha, a city of Galilee, near Jotapata, mentioned by Josephus.`
