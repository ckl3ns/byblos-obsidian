## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Nibshan. i.e. soft or sandy soil; level soft soil, ❅S#5044h. the city of Salt. This city was situated somewhere in the vicinity west of the lake Asphaltites; and supposed by some to be the same as Zoar. Ge 19:22. En-gedi. i.e. fountain of the kid, ❅S#5872h. En-gedi, or Hazazon-Tamar, was situated, according to Eusebius, in the desert west of the Dead Sea. Josephus says it was 300 stadia from Jerusalem, and not far from the lake Asphaltites; and consequently it could not have been far from Jericho and the mouth of the Jordan. It was celebrated for the abundance of its palm trees. Ge 14:7. 1 S 23:29. 24:1. 2 Ch 20:2. SS 1:14. Ezk 47:10.`
