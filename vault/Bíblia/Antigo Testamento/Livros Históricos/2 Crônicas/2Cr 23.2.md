## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`went about. Ps 112:5. Mt 10:16. Ep 5:15. the chief of. Houbigant omits the words of Israel. Bp. Patrick, however, is of opinion that Judah is here called by the general name of Israel: but it is probable, that these “chief of the fathers of Israel,” were the descendants of those priests and Levites, and other pious persons of the ten tribes, who left their cities and possessions, and joined themselves to Judah, in the days of Jeroboam. See the parallel passages. 2 Ch 11:13-17. 1 Ch 15:12. 24:6.`
