## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`there was no passover. Not one on purer principles, more heartily joined in by the people present, more literally or exactly consecrated, according to the law, or more religiously observed. The words do not refer to the number present, but to the manner and spirit. 2 Ch 30:5, 23. 2 K 23:21-23. Ne 8:17. neither did. 2 Ch 30:26, 27. present. Heb. found. 2 Ch +29:29mg. Jg +20:48mg.`
