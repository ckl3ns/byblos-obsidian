## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`took for him. Not for himself, as the Jewish expositors suppose, but for Joash; for Jehoiada’s advanced age renders it highly improbable that he should take them for himself. He was born in the reign of Solomon, and lived through six successive reigns; and must, on any computation, have been upwards of 100 years old when Joash began to reign. See ver. 15. Ge 21:21. 24:4. two wives. Ge +4:19. Mt 19:4-8.`
