## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Zemaraim. Zemaraim could not be, as some have supposed, the same as the hill of Samaria, so called from Shemer, in the days of Omri; but was probably a hill on the confines of Ephraim, near Zemaraim, a city of Benjamin, near Bethel. Ge 10:18. Jsh 18:22. Hear me. 2 Ch 15:2. Jg 9:7.`
