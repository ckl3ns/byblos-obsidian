Many circumstances served to aggravate this barbarous act. Zechariah was a high priest and a prophet, upright and unblameable in the discharge of his high offices; this murder was perpetrated within the very precincts of the courts of the Lord; and this truly good man was by blood the nearest relative of Joash, and the son of the man who had saved him from being murdered, and raised him to the throne!
