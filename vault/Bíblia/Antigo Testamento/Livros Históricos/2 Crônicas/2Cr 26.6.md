## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`warred against. 2 Ch 21:16. Is 14:29. the wall of Gath. 2 S 8:1. 1 Ch 18:1. Jabneh. i.e. God lets build; he will cause to build, ❅S#2996h. Jabneh, or Jamnia, according to Josephus (Ant. l. v. c. 1), was given to the tribe of Dan; and was situated between Lydda and Azotus. It is now called Yebna, which Dr. Wittman describes as “a village about twelve miles distant from Jaffa (Joppa), in a fine open plain, surrounded by hills, and covered by herbage. Northeast of Yebna is a lofty hill, from which is an extensive and pleasing view of Ramnia, distant about five miles. On sloping hills of easy ascent, by which the plains were bordered, Yebna, Ekron, Ashdod, and Askalon were in sight.” about. or, in the country of. 1 S 5:1, 6.`
