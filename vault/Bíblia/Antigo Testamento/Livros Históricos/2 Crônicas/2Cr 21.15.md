## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`by disease. This is supposed to have been a violent dysentery, a disease which is often attended with symptoms similar to those described in the text (Dr. Mead’s Medica Sacra, cap. iv.); by the same death perished Antiochus Epiphanes, and Herod Agrippa. ver. 18, 19.
