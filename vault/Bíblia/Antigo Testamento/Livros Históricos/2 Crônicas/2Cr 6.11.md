## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`I put the ark. 2 Ch 5:7, 10. Ex 40:20. 1 K 8:9, 21. He 9:4. the covenant. As “there was nothing in the ark but the two tables of stone,” consequently they are called the covenant, i.e. a sign of the covenant. ƒ121S. Figure of speech Metonymy of the adjunct, where some circumstance pertaining to the subject is put for the subject itself; here, the sign is put for the thing signified. For other instances of this figure, see Ge +49:10.`
