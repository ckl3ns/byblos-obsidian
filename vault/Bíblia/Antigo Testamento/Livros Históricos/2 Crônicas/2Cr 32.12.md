## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hath not. 2 Ch 31:1. 2 K 18:4, 22. Is 36:7. taken away. This was artfully malicious: many of the people had sacrificed to Jehovah on high places (2 Ch *20:33n. 31:1); and Hezekiah had removed them, as incentives to idolatry. Hence Rabshakeh insinuates that by so doing he had offended Jehovah, deprived the people of their religious rights, and that, consequently, he could neither expect the blessing of God nor the cooperation of the people. Ye shall worship. See on Dt 12:13, 14, 26, 27. one altar. 2 Ch 4:1. Ex 27:1-8. 30:1-6. 40:26-29. 1 K 7:48.`
