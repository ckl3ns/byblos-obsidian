## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Beth-lehem. Bethlehem, called “Bethlehem Judah” (Jg 17:7), to distinguish it from another Bethlehem in Zebulun (Jsh 19:15), and also Ephratah (i.e. fruitful), and by the Arabs, Bait-el-lahm, is situated on a rising ground on the southern side of a deep and extensive valley, and reclining from E. to W., not quite six miles S. of Jerusalem. Ge 35:19. 1 S 17:12. Mt 2:5, 6. Etam. Jg 15:8. 1 Ch 4:32. Tekoa. 2 Ch 20:20. 2 S 14:2. Ne 3:5, 27. Je 6:1. Am 1:1.`
