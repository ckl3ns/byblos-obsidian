## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`two pillars. 1 K 7:15-24. Je 52:20-23. thirty. The Syriac and Arabic have, agreeably to the parallel passage, “eighteen cubits high;” but the Septuagint, Chaldee, and Vulgate have “thirty and five cubits high.” See the Note on 1 K 7:15. high. Heb. long.`
