## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`is not Boaz. Ru 2:20-23. Dt *25:5, 6. He 2:11-14. kindred. Ru 2:1. with whose. Ru 2:8, 23. he winnoweth. It is probable that the winnowing of grain was effected by taking up a portion of the corn in a sieve, and letting it down slowly in the wind; thus the grain would, by its own weight, fall in one place, while the chaff, etc., would be carried to a distance by the wind. It is said here that this was done at night; probably what was threshed out in the day was winnowed in the evening, when the sea breeze set in, which was common in Palestine. Mt 3:12. threshing floor. Ge +50:10. Jg *6:37. 2 S 6:6. 24:16. 2 Ch 3:1.`
