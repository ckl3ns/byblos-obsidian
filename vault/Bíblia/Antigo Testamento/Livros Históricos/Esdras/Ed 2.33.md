## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Lod. i.e. contention, strife. 1 Ch 8:12. Ne 6:2. +7:37. 11:34, 35. Hadid. or, Hadrid, as in some copies. i.e. sharp; making sharp, ❅S#2307h. Ezr 2:33. Ne 7:37. 11:34. Hadid is probably the Adida of Josephus, and the Maccabees (1 Mac 12:38. 13:13), a city situated on a hill in the plain country of Judah, and the Aditha of Eusebius, which he places near Diospolis, Lydda, or Lod. Ono. i.e. strong; gain-bringing; strength, power; his vigor, his iniquity, ❅S#207h. Ezr 2:33. 1 Ch 8:12. Ne 6:2. 7:37. 11:35.`
