## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Blessed. There is a most amiable spirit of piety in these reflections. Instead of expatiating on the praises of his munificent patron, or boasting of his own services, he blesses God for “putting such a thing in the king’s heart;” and for all the assistance and favor shown him by the king and his counselors. Ezr 6:22. 1 Ch 29:10, etc. Ph 4:10. put such. Ezr 6:22. Ne 2:12. 7:5. 2 Co 8:16. He 8:10. 10:16. Ja 1:17. Re 17:17. in the king’s heart. Ne 2:8. Pr *21:1. to beautify. Is 60:13.`
