## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`children. The word children, in this table, when prefixed to the name of a man, signifies the descendants of that person, as from ver. 3-21; and when prefixed to the name of a town, place, etc., it signifies the inhabitants of that place, as from ver. 21-25. Parosh. i.e. dancing; a flea, ❅S#6551h. Ezr 2:3. 8:3, Pharosh. 10:25. Ne 3:25. 7:8. 10:14. (1) One whose children came up from Babylon, Ezr 2:3. (2) One whose sons married strange wives, Ezr 10:25. (3) Father of Pedaiah, who helped to build the wall, Ne 3:25.`
