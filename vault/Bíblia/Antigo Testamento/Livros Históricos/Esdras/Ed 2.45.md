## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Lebanah. i.e. white, the moon, ❅S#3838h. Ezr 2:45. Ne 7:48. These variations merely arise from the mutation of hay into aleph, according to the Chaldee dialect; the original being respectively “Lebanah” and “Lebana;” “Hagabah” and “Hagaba.” Ne 7:48, Lebana, Hagaba. Hagabah. i.e. a locust; grasshopper, leaper,❅S#2286h. Ezr 2:45. Ne 7:48. Akkub. i.e. taken by the heel. 1 Ch +3:24.`
