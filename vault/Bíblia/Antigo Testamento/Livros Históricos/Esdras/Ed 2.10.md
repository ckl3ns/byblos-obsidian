## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Bani. i.e. built up, ❅S#1137h. Ezr 2:10. 10:29, 34, 38. 2 S 23:36. 1 Ch 6:46. 9:4. Ne 3:17. 8:7. 9:4, 4, 5. 10:13, 14. 11:22. The variation of Bani and Binnui arises from the elision of wav; but the LXX. have here Banoui, as in the parallel place. Ezr 10:34. Ne 7:15, Binnui, 648. (1) One of David’s heroes, 2 S 23:36. (2) A son of Merari, 1 Ch 6:46. (3) A dweller in Jerusalem, 1 Ch 9:4. (4) A Levite; Rehum his son built the wall, Ne 3:17; 9:4, 5. (5) A Levite who lived before the return from the exile, of the sons of Asaph, family of Gershom, Ne 11:22. (6) Founder of a house or family, Ezr 10:34. (7) A Jewish descendant of a strange wife, Ezr 10:38. (8) A priest who assisted Ezra, Ne 10:13, some consider same as (4). (9) A family of exiles, Ezr 2:10; 10:29. (10) One who renewed the covenant under Nehemiah, Ne 10:14, some consider same as (4).`
