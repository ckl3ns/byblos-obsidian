## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`As I happened. lit. “meeting I met” (S#7136h). 2 S 18:9. *20:1. Ex 5:3. Dt *22:6. 1 S +6:9. Je 32:23 (hast caused to come). by chance (S#7122h). Ge +24:44n. Jsh 14:2n. Jg 20:22n. 1 S +6:9. Je 44:23. The story of this young man appears to be wholly a fiction, formed for the purpose of ingratiating himself with David, as the next probable successor to the crown. There is no fact in the case, except the bringing of the diadem and bracelets of Saul, as a sufficient evidence of his death, which, as he appears to have been a plunderer of the slain, he seems to have stripped from the dead body of the unfortunate monarch. It is remarkable, that Saul, who had forfeited his crown by his disobedience and ill-timed clemency with respect to the Amalekites, should now have the insignia of royalty stripped from his person by one of those very people. Ru 2:3. 1 S 6:9. Lk 10:31. mount. ver. 21. 1 S 28:4. 31:1. Saul. 1 S 31:2-7.`
