## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Thou shalt. 2 S *21:17. if we flee. 2 S 17:2. 1 K 22:31. Zc 13:7. care for us. Heb. set their heart on us. 2 S 13:20. but now. The particle attah, “now,” is doubtless a mistake for the pronoun attah, “thou:” and so it appears to have been read by the LXX., Vulgate, and Chaldee, and by two of Kennicott’s and De Rossi’s MSS. worth, etc. Heb. as ten thousand of us. SS 5:10. La 4:20. succor. Heb. be to succor. 2 S 10:11. 11:1. Ex 17:10-12.`
