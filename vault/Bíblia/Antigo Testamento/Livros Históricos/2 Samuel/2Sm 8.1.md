## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2964. B.C. 1040. An. Ex. Is 451. And after. 2 S 7:9. 21:15-22. Metheg-ammah. or, the bridle of Ammah. i.e. the bridle of the arm, ❅S#4965h, only here. 2 S 2:24. 1 Ch 18:1, etc., Gath. In the parallel passage of Chronicles, we read, “David took Gath and her towns;” and it is probable that Gath and its districts were called Metheg-ammah in David’s time; which, being unusual or becoming obsolete, in the time of the author of the Chronicles, led him thus to explain it.`
