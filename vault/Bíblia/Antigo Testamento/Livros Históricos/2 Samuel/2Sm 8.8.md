## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Betah. i.e. confidence; security; refuge, ❅S#984h, only here. In 1 Ch 18:8 it is called Tibhath, i.e. slaughter. Probably the same as Bathne in Syria, between Beroaea and Hierapolis. 1 Ch 18:8, Tibhath. Berothai. i.e. my wells, ❅S#1268h. 1 Ch 18:8, Chun. Ezk 47:16h, Berothah. Berothai is probably the Barathena of Ptolemy, which he mentions, along with Sabe, as a city of Arabia Deserta, in the confines of the Palmyrenian district. exceeding. 1 Ch 22:14, 16. 29:7. 2 Ch 4:1-18.`
