## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Abel. Or rather, probably, Abel of Beth-Maachah, as in the next verse. It appears, from Joab having marched “through all the tribes of Israel,” to have been situated in the northern confines of the land of Israel, and in the half tribe of Manasseh, east of Jordan, as that was the situation of Maachah, to which it belonged. This agrees with the situation of the Abela which Eusebius and Jerome place between Paneas, or Caesarea Philippi, and Damascus. Josephus (Ant. I. vii. c. 10) says it was a fortified city, and a metropolis of the Israelites; and (Ant. I. viii. c. 6) also that it belonged to the ten tribes, having been taken from the king of Damascus. Jsh 18:21, 25. 1 K 15:20. 2 K 15:29. 2 Ch 16:4. Bethmaachah. i.e. house of oppression, ❅S#1038h. ver. 15. 2 K 15:29. Berites. ❅S#1276h, only here. Jsh 18:25, Beeroth.`
