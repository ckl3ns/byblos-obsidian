## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`in some place. 1 S 23:23. we will. Ps 12:2. 2 P 2:18. light upon. This is a very beautiful and expressive figure. The dew in Palestine and other warm climates falls fast, sudden, and heavy; and it falls upon every spot of earth, so that not a blade of grass escapes it. It is therefore no inept emblem of a numerous and active army; and it was, perhaps, for this reason that the Romans called their light armed forces rorarii. 1 K 20:10. 2 K 18:23. 19:24. Is 10:13, 14. Ob 3. dew. Dt 32:2. Ps 133:3. Pr 19:12. Is 18:4. Ho 14:5. Mi 5:7.`
