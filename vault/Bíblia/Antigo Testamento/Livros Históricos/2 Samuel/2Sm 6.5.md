## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`David. 1 S 10:5. 16:16. 2 K 3:15. 1 Ch *13:8. 15:10-24. Ps 47:5. 68:25-27. 150:3-5. Da 3:5, 7, 10, 15. Am 5:23. 6:5. all. *ƒ171A, Ex +9:6. played. 1 S 18:7. 1 Ch 13:8. 15:29. on all manner. This place should doubtless be corrected from the parallel place, 1 Ch 13:8; where, instead of bechol atzey beroshim, which is literally, “with all trees or wood of fir,” we read bechol oz oovesheerim, “with all their might, and with songs.” This makes good sense, while the former makes none: the LXX. have the same reading here, en isxui, kai en odais. But see following note on the figure of speech involved here. fir wood. ƒ121D1. Metonymy of the Cause B557: Trees are put for arms or instruments made from them. For another instance of this figure see Na 2:3. harps. Ge 4:21. 31:27. 1 S 10:5. psalteries. 1 S 10:5. timbrels. Ge 31:27. cymbals. Ps 150:5.`
