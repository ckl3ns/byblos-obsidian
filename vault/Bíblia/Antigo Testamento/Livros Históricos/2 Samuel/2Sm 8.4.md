## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`from him. or, of his. chariots. As 1 Ch 18:4. See another elipsis supplied from Chronicles at 2 S 5:8. seven hundred. In the parallel place in Chronicles it is “seven thousand horsemen,” a far more probable number. The letter zayin, with a dot upon it, stands for seven thousand, and the final letter, noon, for seven hundred: the great similarity of these letters might easily cause the one to be mistaken for the other, and so produce an error in this place. David houghed. Dt 17:16. Jsh *11:6, 9. Ps 20:7. 33:16, 17. reserved. 1 K 10:26.`
