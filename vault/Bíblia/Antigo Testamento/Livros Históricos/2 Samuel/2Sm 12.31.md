## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`and put them. Rather, as the Hebrew particle used here frequently signifies, “And he put them to saws, and to harrows, and to axes,” etc. as we say “to put a person to the plow, to the anvil, to the last,” etc. See on 1 Ch 20:3. 2 S 8:2. Ps 21:8, 9. Am 1:3. pass through. Je 43:11. Mt 25:32. brickkiln. Je *43:9n. Rather, a platform upon which the king sat upon his throne and reviewed the captives, and decided upon their destiny. Mt 25:31-33.`
