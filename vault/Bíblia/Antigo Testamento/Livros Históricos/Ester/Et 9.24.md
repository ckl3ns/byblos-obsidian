## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the enemy. or, adversary. ver. 10. Est 3:5-13. 7:6. Pur. The word pur seems to be derived either from the Persian bahr and bar, a part, portion, lot, or pari, any thing which happens fortuitously or fortunately; whence the annual festival in commemoration of the wonderful deliverance of the Jews was called Purim, or in Arabic and Persian, Fuhr, or Lots; which has been observed by them, in all places of their dispersion, from that day to the present time, without any interruption. Est 3:7. Ge ◐24:44n. consume. Heb. crush. Ex 14:24 (troubled). 23:27 (destroy). Dt 2:15. Je 51:34.`
