## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`If it please the king. Esther had probably been informed by Mordecai, that there were still many enemies of the Jews who sought their destruction, who had escaped the preceding day; and therefore begged that the second day might be added to the former permission; and that the sons of Haman, who had already been slain, might be suspended on gibbets, as a terror to those who sought the destruction of the Jews. according unto. Est 8:11. let Haman’s ten sons be hanged. Heb. let men hang Haman’s ten sons. Dt 21:23. 2 S 21:6, 9. Ga 3:13.`
