The LXX. read, triakosious kai pente andras, “three hundred and five men;” and Josephus, “three hundred and eighty-five men.” Probably the eighty-five were priests, and the three hundred the families of the priests; three hundred and eighty-five being the whole population of Nob. 1 S 2:30-33, 36. 3:12-14. a linen ephod. 1 S 2:18, 28. Ex 28:40. 39:27.`
