## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Philistine. or, Philistines. the women. Ex 15:20. Jg 11:34. Ps 68:25. Je 31:11-13. instruments of music. Heb. three stringed instruments. The original shalishim is rendered by the Vulgate sistris. The sistrum was an ancient Egyptian instrument made of brass, with three, and sometimes more brass rods across; which, being loose in their holes, made a jingling noise when shaken.`
