## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the Hebrews. Probably such as they held in bondage, or who were their servants. Instead of haivrim, ‘the Hebrews,’ the LXX. evidently read haavdim, for they have oi douloi, ‘the slaves;’ but this reading is not countenanced by any other version, nor by any MS. 1 S 29:4. Jg 7:23.`
