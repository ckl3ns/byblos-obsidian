## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`his men. ver. 13. slew. Jg 14:19. 2 S *3:14. Is 43:1, 4. two hundred men. The Septuagint has only ekaton andras, “one hundred men”; and as Saul covenanted for an hundred, and as David himself says (2 S 3:14), that he espoused Michal for an hundred, it is very probable that this is the true reading.`
