## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Nabal. i.e. folly; foolish, ❅S#5037h. ver. 4, 5, 9, 10, 14, 19, 25, 25, 26, 34, 36, 36, 37, 38, 39, 39, 39. 27:3. 30:5. 2 S 2:2. 3:3. Abigail. i.e. cause of delight; father of joy; source of joy, ❅S#26h. ver. 14, 18, 23, 32, 36, 39, 40, 42. 27:3. 30:5. 2 S 2:2. 3:3. 17:25. 1 Ch 2:16, 17. 3:1. (1) The wife of Nabal of Carmel, and afterward of David. (2) A sister or a niece of Zeruiah and mother of Amasa, 2 S 17:25; 1 Ch 2:16, 17. good. Pr 14:1. 31:26, 30, 31. understanding. ❅S#7922h: 1 S 25:3. 1 Ch 22:12. 26:14. 2 Ch 2:12. 30:22. Ezr 8:18. Ne 8:8. Jb 17:4. Ps 111:10. Pr 3:4. 12:8. 13:15. 16:22. 19:11. 23:9. Da 8:25. beautiful countenance. Ge 29:17. was churlish. ver. 10, 11, 17. Ps 10:3. Is 32:5-7. and he was. Wehoo calibbee, literally, “and he was a Calebite;” but as the word cailev signifies a dog, the Septuagint has understood it as implying a man of a canine disposition, and translated it, kai anthropos kunikos, “and he was a doggish man.” It is understood in the same way by the Syriac and Arabic.`
