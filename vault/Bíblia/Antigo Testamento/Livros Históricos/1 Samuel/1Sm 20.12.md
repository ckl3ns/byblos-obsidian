## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`O Lord. This verse is evidently deficient. The LXX. have Kurios o theos Israal oiden, “The Lord God of Israel doth know;” the Syriac and Arabic, “The Lord God of Israel is witness;” either of which makes a good sense. But two of Dr. Kennicott’s MSS. supply the word chai, liveth; and the text reads thus: “As the Lord God of Israel liveth, when I have sounded my father, if there be good unto David, and I then send not unto thee,” etc.; which is a still better sense. Jsh 22:22. Jg 6:22. Jb 31:4. Ps 17:3. 139:1-4. Jn 20:28. sounded. Heb. searched. Pr 20:5. 25:2, 3. show it thee. Heb. uncover thine ear. ver. 2. Ru +4:4mg.`
