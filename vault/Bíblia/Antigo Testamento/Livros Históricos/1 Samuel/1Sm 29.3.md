## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Is not this David. These words seem to mark no definite time; and may be understood thus: “Is not this David, the servant of Saul the king of Israel, who has been with me for a considerable time?” these days. 1 S 27:7. found. 1 S 25:28. Da 6:5. Jn 19:6. Ro 12:17. 1 P 3:16.`
