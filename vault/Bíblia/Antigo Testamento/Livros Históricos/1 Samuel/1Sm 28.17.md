## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`to him. or, for himself. The LXX. read soi, and the Vulgate tibi, “to thee”; which is the reading of five of Dr. Kennicott’s and De Rossi’s MSS., as well as both the Bibles printed in Venice in 1518, where we read lecha, “to thee,” for lo, “to him;” and as the words are spoken to Saul, this seems to be evidently the correct reading. Pr 16:4. as he spake. 1 S 13:13, 14. 15:27-29. me. Heb. mine hand. ver. 15. thy neighbor. 1 S 15:28. 16:13. 24:20. hath rent. 1 S 15:28. 1 K 11:31.`
