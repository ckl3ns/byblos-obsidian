## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`an enemy, etc. or, the affliction of the tabernacle, for all the wealth which God would have given Israel. This appears to be the right translation; for agreeably to this prediction, he did see the tabernacle deprived of the ark, which was its glory, and lived to hear that it was captured by the Philistines. 1 S 4:4, 11, 22. Ps 78:59-64. an old man. Zc 8:4.`
