## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Beth-car. i.e. house of Car or a lamb, ❅S#1033h. This place was probably situated in the tribe of Dan. Josephus calls it korraioi; The LXX. Baithxor; Targum, Beth-saron; Syriac and Arabic, Beth-jasan; by which Houbigant supposes is meant Beth-shan.`
