## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the winterhouse. A warm apartment suited to the season of the year (December, when snow is often upon the ground in Palestine), in which was a pan or brazier (ach, or ikhkh, as it is pronounced in Arabic) of burning charcoal; for we learn from Bp. Pococke (vol. i. p. 87) and Dr. Russel (vol. i. pp. 82, 85), that this was the mode in which the Orientals warmed their apartments. Je 22:14-16. Jg 3:20. Am *3:15.`
