## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Cir. A.M. 3417. B.C. 587. The word. Dahler supposes that this prophecy was delivered about a year after the taking of Jerusalem; which appears highly probable. Dr. Blayney justly supposes this and the following chapter to refer to the future restoration of the Jews and Israelites in the times of the Gospel; though also touching at the restoration from the Babylonian captivity. The ninth verse is expressly referred to the times of the Messiah by the Chaldee paraphrast; who renders, “They shall serve before Jehovah their God, and shall obey Messiah the son of David, whom I shall raise up to them.” Je 1:1, 2. 26:15.`
