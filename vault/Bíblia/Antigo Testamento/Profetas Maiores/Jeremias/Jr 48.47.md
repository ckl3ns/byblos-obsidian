## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Yet will I bring. Many of the Moabites were afterwards restored to their country by Cyrus, as we learn from Josephus; but they were never restored to their national consequence; and perhaps their restoration in the latter days refers to the conversion of their scattered remnants to the gospel. The Moabites are one of several nations promised to be regathered in the latter days: Elam, Je 49:39; Ammon, Je 49:6; Israel, Je 46:27, 28. Moab and Ammon were the children of Lot. Je 46:26. 49:6, 39. Is *18:7. *19:18-23. 23:18. Ezk 16:+*49, 53-55, 60. in the latter. See on Je 23:20. 30:24. Nu 24:14. Dt 4:30. 31:29. Jb +*19:25. Ezk 38:8. Da 2:28. 10:14. Ho 3:5.`
