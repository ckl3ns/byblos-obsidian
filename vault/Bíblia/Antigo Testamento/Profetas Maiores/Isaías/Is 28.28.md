## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Bread. ƒ121H, Ps +74:15. By Metonymy of the Effect, the thing made is put for the material from which it is made or produced. Here, “bread is bruised” signifies the corn of which it is made. The sense is clear from verse 27 and Job 28:5. In Ps 104:14, we have the opposite of this in the Metonomy of the Cause (ƒ121D14). Is 21:10. Jb 28:5. Ps ◐104:14. Am 9:9. Mt 3:12. 13:37-43.
