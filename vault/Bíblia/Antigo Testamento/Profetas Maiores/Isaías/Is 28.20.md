## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the bed. ƒ7. Allegory as repeated Hypocatastasis B750, Ga +4:24. Here only one part of the figure is mentioned, the bed and its covering, and not the people to whom it refers. The prophet is speaking of the great fear which ought to agitate the people of Judea at the speedy coming of Sennacherib; but they preferred to be left in their false security. By this beautiful allegorical illustration they are informed that their rest should be restless, and their sleep should be soon disturbed. Is 57:12, 13. 59:5, 6. *64:6. 66:3-6. Je 7:8-10. Mt 5:20. Ro 9:30-32. 1 Co 1:18-31. Ph 3:8, 9.`
