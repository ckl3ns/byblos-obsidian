## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`I will bring. Ezk 21:31. 28:7. Ps 106:41. Je 4:7. 12:12. Hab 1:6-10. they shall. That is, “the Chaldeans shall possess the houses of the Jews.” The antecedents of pronouns are thus frequently understood in Hebrew poetry. Je 6:12. La 5:2. I will also. Ezk 33:28. Is 5:14. the pomp. That is, the magnificence of their greatest and haughtiest princes. their holy places shall be defiled. or, they shall inherit their holy places. Ezk 21:2. 22:16. Jsh 14:1. 2 K 24:20. 25:8, 9. 2 Ch 7:19. Ps 83:12.`
