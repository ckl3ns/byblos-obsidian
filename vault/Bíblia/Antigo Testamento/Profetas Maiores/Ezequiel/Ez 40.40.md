## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`at the side. Two tables were on each side, as you come into the porch of the gate; and two on each side of the inner part of the gate that looked towards the altar; in all eight tables, on which they slew and cut up the victims. It does not appear that any such tables were used either in the tabernacle or temple; and this seems to intimate the introduction of a new and more spiritual dispensation. See the references on ver. 38, 39. Ezk 41:22. as one goeth up. or, at the step. ver. 35. north gate. Le 1:11.`
