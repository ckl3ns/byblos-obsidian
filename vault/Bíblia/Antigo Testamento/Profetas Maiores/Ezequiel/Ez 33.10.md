The impenitent Jews seem to have charged the prophet’s messages with inconsistency: for whilst he warned them to repent, and assured the penitent of forgiveness, he also predicted that the people “would pine away in their transgressions.” The prediction, however, merely implied that God foresaw that the people in general would be impenitent, though some individuals would repent and be pardoned.
