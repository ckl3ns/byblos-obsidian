## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Doth he not speak. Is it not his usual custom to deal in enigmas? His figures are not to be understood; we should not trouble ourselves with them. God therefore commands the prophet to declare, in the next chapter, the same things in the plainest terms, so that they should not complain of his parables. Mt *13:11, 13-15. Jn 16:25. Ac 17:18. parables. Nu +21:27. 23:7n.`
