## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`dross. Ps 66:10. Is 1:25. Zc 13:9. Ml *3:2, 3. I will. Jerusalem is here represented as the fining pot; all the people, who had become dross, are to be gathered together in it; and the fire of the Chaldeans, blown by the wrath of God, is to melt the whole. No ordinary means will avail to purge their impurities; the most violent must therefore be resorted to. Ezk 11:7. 24:3-6. Mi 4:12. Mt 12:30, 40-42.`
