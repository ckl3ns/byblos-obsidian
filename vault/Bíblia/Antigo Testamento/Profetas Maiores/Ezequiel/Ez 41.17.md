## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`To that. That is, the windows were placed above the height of the door, at the east end of the temple, and thus continued, at the same height, and at measured distances, along both sides of the holy place, to the wall of the inner sanctuary, in which there were no windows. measure. Heb. measures. Ezk 42:15.`
