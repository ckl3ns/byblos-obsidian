## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Before. Perhaps this means that the north door was 100 cubits from the entrance into the court; and that the doorway, or portico, was 50 cubits in length; or, that it faced one of the cloisters, the length of which was 100 cubits, and its breadth 50, which was the proportion of all the cloisters.`
