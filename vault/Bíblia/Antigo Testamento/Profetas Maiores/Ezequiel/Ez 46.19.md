## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the entry. This entry was at the west side of the north gate of the inner court (see Ezk 44:4, 5). Ezk 40:44-46. 42:9. a place. This place was at the west corners of the inner court, or court of the priests; where they prepared the most holy things, the trespass and sin offering, which none but the priests might eat; that they might not “bear them out into the utter court, to sanctify the people,” that is, by touching them incapacitate them from discharging their ordinary occupations.`
