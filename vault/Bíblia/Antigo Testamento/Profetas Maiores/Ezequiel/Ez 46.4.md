## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the burnt. The proportions of the burnt offerings, and also of the meat and drink offerings, are very different here from those prescribed in the Mosaic law. The meat offering, under the law, was only three tenths of an epha to a bullock, two tenths for a ram, and one tenth for a lamb, with the fourth part of a hin of oil. Ezk 45:17. Nu 28:9, 10.`
