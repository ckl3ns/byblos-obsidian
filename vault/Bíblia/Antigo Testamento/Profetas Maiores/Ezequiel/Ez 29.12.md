## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`desolate in. Ezk 30:7. Je 25:15-19. 27:6-11. Na 3:8, 9. and I will scatter. We learn from Berosus (apud. Josephus, l. ix. c. 11, sec. 1) that Nebuchadnezzar sent several captive Egyptians to Babylon; and from Megasthenes (apud. Euseb. Praep. Evang. l. ix. c. 41) that he transplanted others to Pontus; and it is probable, that at the dissolution of the Babylonian empire, about forty years after, Cyrus permitted them to return to their native country. Ezk 30:23, 26. Je 46:19.`
