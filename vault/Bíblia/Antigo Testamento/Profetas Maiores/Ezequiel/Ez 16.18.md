## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`And tookest. This seems to intimate that the Israelites not only spent their own wealth and abundance in building and decorating idol temples, and in maintaining their worship, but that they made use of the holy vestments, and the various kinds of offerings which belonged to Jehovah, in order to honor and serve the idols of the heathen. ver. 10. Ho *2:8.`
