Probably the valley near the sea of Gennesareth, as the Targum renders, and so called because it was the great road by which the merchants and traders from Syria and other Eastern countries went into Egypt. Perhaps what is now called the plains of Haouran, south of Damascus. graves. Heb. qeber, Ge 23:4. Ps *49:14. Is 34:1-3. on the east. Ezk 47:18. Nu 34:11. Lk 5:1. Jn 6:1. noses. or, mouths. Hamon-gog. that is, The multitude of Gog. Nu 11:34mg. Je +10:13mg.`
