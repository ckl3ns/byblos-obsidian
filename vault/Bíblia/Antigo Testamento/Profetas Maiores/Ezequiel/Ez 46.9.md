## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`when the people. This may intimate, that every thing should be regulated, in divine worship, so as to prevent disorder and interruption, and also that men should go forward and make progress in religion, and not turn their backs upon God. 1 Co 14:40. come before. Ex 23:14-17. 34:23. Dt 16:16. Ps 84:7. Ml 4:4. solemn feasts. Ge +17:21h. Le *23:2n. he that entereth. Ezk 1:12, 17. Ph 3:13, 14. He 10:38. 2 P 2:20, 21.`
