## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`robbers of thy people. Heb. children of robbers. or, destroyers. lit. “breakers forth.” 1 K 12:15n. Ps 17:4. Ezk 18:10mg. exalt themselves. Ac 4:25-28. Re 17:17. to establish. Ac 13:27. but they shall fall. Thus it is that the book of Daniel was understood in this time, and some of the Jews undertook to fulfill the prophecy, but failed. It never works for men to try to help God out, as in the case of Rebekah, Ge 27:8, etc. This shows the impossibility of men to endeavor to fulfill prophecy, even when they appear to find themselves in the right circumstances to do so. Equally impossible is it for men or Satan to thwart the fulfillment of prophecy. Is 41:4, ✓22, ✓23. 26. 43:9, *13. 44:7. 45:21. 46:10. 48:14.`
