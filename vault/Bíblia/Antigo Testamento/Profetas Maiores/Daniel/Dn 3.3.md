## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the princes. Achashdarpenaya, rendered lieutenants in Est 3:12, etc., probably chief satraps or viceroys, from the Persian achash, great, eminent, and sitrab, a satrap. Ps ch. 82. Ac 19:34, 35. Ro 1:21-28. 3:11. 1 Co 1:24-26. Re 13:13-16. 17:13, 17. the governors. Signaya, in Persian, shagnah, deputies or lieutenants. ver. 3, 27. Da 2:48. 6:7. captains. Pacawatha, governors of provinces. Ne 5:14, 18. See Est 3:13. Hg 1:14. the judges. Adargazraya, chief judges, or senators, from adar, great, and Chald. gezar, to judge, decree. the treasurers. Gedavraya, written gizzavraya, Ezr 7:21, treasurers, from the Persian gunjvar. the counsellors. Dethavraya, counsellors, judges, from dath, in Persian, dad, law, and var, possessor or guardian. the sheriffs. Tiphtaya, probably the same as the Arabic Mufti, or head officer of law.`
