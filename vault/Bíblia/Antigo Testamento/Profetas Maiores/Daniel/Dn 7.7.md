Da *2:40. 8:10. 2 S 22:43. Re *13:1-10. and stamped. Re 6:16, 17. 14:19. 16:1. 19:15-21. and it had ten. See on ver. 24. Da *2:41, 42, 44. Re 12:3. *13:1. ✓17:7, 12. The ten horns, seen as contemporaneous, not successive to each other, represent ten kings (ver. 24) at some time yet future, among whom one will arise, uprooting three in the process, who will then rule over all.`
