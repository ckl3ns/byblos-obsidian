## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`unawares to Laban. Heb. the heart of Laban. See references on ver. 27. ƒ171Q20. Figure of speech Synecdoche of the Part B648. The heart is put for the whole man, in respect to his knowledge or affection. Here, Jacob baffled Laban’s knowledge by hiding his intentions. For other instances of this figure see ver. 26mg, 27. 2 S 15:6. Lk 21:34.`
