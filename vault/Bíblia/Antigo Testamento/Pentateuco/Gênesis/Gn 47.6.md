## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`is. ver. 11. Ge 13:9. 20:15. 34:10. 45:18-20. Pr 21:1. Jn 17:2. Goshen. ver. 4, 11. men of activity. Anshey chayil, men of strength, power, ability, or prowess. It implies both fitness of mind and body; and so valor, prudence, diligence, and virtue. Pr +*22:3, *29. rulers. Ex +*18:21. 1 S 21:7. 1 Ch 27:29-31. 2 Ch 26:10. Pr 22:29. cattle. The word signifies property or possession of any kind, though it frequently is used for cattle, because in ancient times they constituted the principal part of a man’s possessions. See ver. 16. Ex 9:3-6, 10-21.`
