## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`fulfilled her week. The public marriage feast made on this occasion (ver. 22), seems to have formed the regular method of recognizing the marriage, and lasted seven days: it would therefore have been improper to have broken off the solemnities to which all the men of the place had been invited (ver. 22) and probably Laban wished to keep the fraud from the public eye. It is perfectly plain that Jacob did not serve seven years more before he got Rachel to wife. Jg *14:12. Rachel. i.e. an ewe, ❅S#7354h. ver. 6, 9, 10, 11, 12, 16, 17, 18, 18, 20, 25, 29, 30, 30, 31.
