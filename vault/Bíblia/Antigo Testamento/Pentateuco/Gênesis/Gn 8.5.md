This figure is present in the Hebrew, but is not observable in the English translation, either here or in the other instances of this figure at Ex 8:15. 2 S 3:18. 1 K 22:30. 2 Ch 18:29. 31:10. Ps 8:1. *32:9 (where it is literally “not to understand,” or “having no understanding”: note the infinitive construction “to understand” for the indicative). 77:1. Pr 12:6. Is 5:5. 38:16. 49:7.
