## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`made it a law. From this history, and from Diodorus Siculus (lib. i.), we learn that the land of Egypt was divided into three parts: one belonged to the priests (ver. 22, 26); a second was the king’s (which appears to have been the land of Rameses, or Goshen, ver. 11); the remainder was the subjects’. Now Joseph, having purchased the land of the people (ver. 19, 20), restored it, on the condition of their paying a fifth part of the produce to the king, beyond which he appears to have made no demand. unto this day. Ge +19:38. except. ver. 22. Ezk 7:24. priests. or, princes. See on ver. 22.`
