## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`wash your feet. In those ancient times, shoes such as ours, were not in use; and the foot was protected only with sandals or soles, fastened round the foot with straps. It was, therefore, not only necessary from motives of cleanliness, but also a very great refreshment, in so hot a country, to get the feet washed at the end of a day’s journey; and this is the first thing that Abraham proposes. Ge 19:2. 24:32. 43:24. Jg 19:21. 1 S 25:41. 2 S 11:8. Lk 7:44. Jn 13:5-15. 1 Ti 5:10. rest. Mk 6:31. Jn 6:10. tree. Rest in the shade was the second requisite for the refreshment of a weary traveler.`
