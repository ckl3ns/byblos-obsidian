## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Hadar. or, Hadad. More than 300 MSS. and printed editions read Hadad, as in 1 Ch 1:30. Tema. i.e. south desert; southerner, ❅S#8485h. 1 Ch 5:19. Jb ◐2:11. 6:19. Is 21:14. Je 25:23. (1) One of the sons of Ishmael, Ge 25:15; 1 Ch 1:30. (2) An Ishmaelite tribe in northern Arabia descended from Tema, son of Ishmael. (3) The name Tema is also applied to the country occupied by that tribe, Jb 6:19; Is 21:14; Je 25:23. Jetur. i.e. an enclosure; defence, ❅S#3195h. 1 Ch 1:31. 5:19. Compare Lk 3:1, Ituraea. Naphish. i.e. refreshed; recreation; taking breath, ❅S#5305h. 1 Ch 1:31. 5:19. Kedemah. i.e. eastward, ❅S#6929h. 1 Ch 1:31.`
