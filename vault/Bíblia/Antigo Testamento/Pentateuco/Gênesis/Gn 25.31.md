`this day. ƒ160B. Figure of speech Simile; or, Resemblance B728: a declaration that one thing resembles another; or, comparison by resemblance. Sometimes a Simile is really used as a figure, implying not merely a resemblance but the actual thing itself. Here, “Sell me as on this day,” means ‘on this very day’ (Dt +4:26).
