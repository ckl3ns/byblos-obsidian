## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`week. Thomas Scott notes that the week, here mentioned, was that of the marriage feast; and did not relate to the years Jacob afterwards served. The division of time by weeks intimates that some regard was paid to the sabbath (See related notes, Ge 2:3n. 4:3n. Ex 16:26n. Is 58:13n). Ge 2:2, 3. 8:10-12. Le 18:18. Jg 14:10, 12. Ml 2:15. Mt 19:5. 1 Ti 6:10. we will. ver. 20. ƒ96D4.
