## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Salah. Heb. Shelah. i.e. sent, shooting forth; a missile, ❅S#7974h. Ge 11:12-15. 1 Ch 1:18, 18, 24. Lk 3:35. Eber. i.e. the region beyond; a shoot, ❅S#5677h. ver. 21, 25. Ge 11:14, 15, 16, 17. 14:13. Nu 24:24. Jsh 24:2, 3, 14, 15. 1 Ch 1:18, 19, 25. 5:13. 8:12, 22. Ne 12:20. Lk 3:35. (1) The father of Peleg and the progenitor of the race of the Hebrews, Ge 10:21; 1 Ch 1:19. (2) The word is apparently used denoting, it may be, the Hebrews (descendants of Eber), but it may also be understood as referring to ‘those beyond the river’ (Euphrates), Nu 24:24. (3) A head of a family in Gad, 1 Ch 5:13. (4) A son of Elpaal, descendant of Benjamin, 1 Ch 8:12. (5) Apparently a son of Shashak, 1 Ch 8:22. (6) A priest of the family of Amok, Ne 12:20.`
