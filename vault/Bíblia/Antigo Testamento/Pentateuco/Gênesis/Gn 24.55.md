## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a few days. or, a full year, or ten months. Ge +4:3mg. Le 25:29. Jg 14:8. ƒ171T2C. Figure of speech Synecdoche (of the Part); or, Transfer B654: the exchange of one idea for another associated idea; of the part, where a part of the time is put for the whole time, as here, the plural “days” is put for a whole year. For other instances of this figure see Ge 40:4. Ex 13:10. Le 25:29. Jg 11:40. 17:10. 21:19. 1 S 1:3. 27:7. 1 K 17:7mg, 15mg. 18:1. Am 4:4mg.`
