## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 687. B.C. 3317. Enoch. i.e. dedicated; teacher, ❅S#2585h. Ge 4:17, 17, 18. 5:18, 19, 21, 22, 23, 24. 25:4. 46:9. Ex 6:14. Nu 26:5. 1 Ch 1:3, 33. 5:3. (1) A son of Cain, Ge 4:17, 18. (2) The name given by Cain to a city which he built, Ge 4:17. (3) The son of Jared and father of Methuselah, Ge 5:18, 21. He is called in Ju 14 “the seventh from Adam” to distinguish him from Enoch the son of Cain. He is said to have “walked with God,” Ge 5:22, and was translated to heaven, for “God took him,” Ge 5:24. Methuselah. i.e. man of dart, or he dies and it is sent—namely, the flood. ❅S#4968h. ver. 21, 22, 25, 26, 27. 1 Ch 1:3. The son of Enoch and grandfather of Noah, Ge 5:27; 1 Ch 1:3. He was the longest-lived man, and died at the age of nine hundred and sixty-nine years. Gr. Mathusala. Lk 3:37.`
