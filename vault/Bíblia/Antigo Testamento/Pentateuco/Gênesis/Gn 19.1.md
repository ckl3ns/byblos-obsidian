## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`And there came two angels. Or, rather, ‘the two angels came,’ referring to those mentioned in the preceding chapter, and there called ‘men.’ It seems (from Ge 18:22) that these two angels were sent to Sodom, while the third, who was the Lord or Jehovah, remained with Abraham. Ge 18:1-3, 16, 22. sat. Ps 1:1. Pr 1:14. Jn 18:18. gate. Ge +14:7. Est 2:19. Pr 31:23. rose. Ge 18:1-5. Jb 31:32. He 13:2. bowed. Ge 18:2. 1 S 24:8.`
