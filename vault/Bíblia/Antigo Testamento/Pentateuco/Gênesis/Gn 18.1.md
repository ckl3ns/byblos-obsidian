In these verses we have a delightful picture of genuine and primitive hospitality: a venerable father sits at the tent door, not only to enjoy the current of refreshing air, but that if he saw any weary and exhausted travelers, he might invite them to rest and refresh themselves during the heat of the day, and the same custom continues in the east.
