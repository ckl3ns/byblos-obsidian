## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`two young. ver. 11, 19. Le 23:18, 19. Bishop Patrick observes that no peace offerings are appointed in this chapter, which were chiefly for the benefit of the offerers, and therefore in them they were left more to themselves; but burnt offerings, which were purely for the honor of God, and confessions of his dominion, and which figured evangelical piety and devotion, by which the soul is wholly offered up to God, in the flames of holy love; and sin offerings, which were typical of Christ’s sacrifice of himself, by which we and our services are perfected and sanctified. sweet savor. Ep 5:2. seven. Ge 4:15. 8:20, 21. Ps 79:12. Pr 6:31.`
