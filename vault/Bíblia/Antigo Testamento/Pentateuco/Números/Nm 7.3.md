## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`covered wagons. The Hebrew egloth tzav, signifies tilted wagons; so LXX. amaxas lampanikas, with which the Coptic agrees. These were given for the more convenient exporting of the heavier parts of the tabernacle.`
