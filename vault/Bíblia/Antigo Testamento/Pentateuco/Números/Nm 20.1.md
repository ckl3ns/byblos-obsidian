## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A.M. 2552. B.C. 1452. Then. This was the first month of the fortieth year after the departure from Egypt. (Compare Nu 33:38 with ver. 28 of this chapter and Dt 1:3.) This year was the last of their journeyings, for from the going out of the spies (Nu ch. 13) unto this time, was about thirty-eight years. Dt 1:22, 23. 2:14. into. Nu 13:21. 27:14. 33:36. Dt 32:51. Kadesh. This Kadesh in the wilderness of Zin, is different from Kadesh-barnea, lying in, or adjoining to the wilderness of Paran, about eight leagues south of Hebron (See Nu 34:3, 4. Jsh 15:1, 3). Kadesh is called Rekam by the Targumists, Rekem, in the Syriac, and Rakim in the Arabic. Rekem is on the east, meaning of the land of Israel. ver. 16. Ps 29:8. Miriam. Nu 12:1, 10, 15. 26:59. Ex 2:4, 7. 15:20. Mi 6:4.`
