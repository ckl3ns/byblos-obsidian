## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`He shall purify. Yithchatta, literally, “he shall sin himself,” i.e. not add sin, but take it away—purify. So we say to “fleece,” and to “skin,” which do not signify to “add a fleece,” or a “skin,” but to take one away. ver. 17, 18. Ps 51:7. Is 1:16. Ezk 36:25. Ac 15:9. 24:16. 2 Co 1:12. 1 Th 4:3, 4. 1 Ti 3:9. Ja 1:27. 1 J 3:3. Re 7:14. third day. Nu 31:19. Ex 19:11, 15. Le 7:17. Ho 6:2. 1 Co +✓15:3, 4.`
