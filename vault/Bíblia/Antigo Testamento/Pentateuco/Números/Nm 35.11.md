## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`ye shall appoint. As the goel, or kinsman, had a right to avenge the death of his relation, by slaying the murderer wherever he found him, the appointment of these cities was a humane institution for the protection of the involuntary homicide; for they were designed only for the protection of such. See on ver. 6. Jsh 20:2. refuge. Pr 14:26. He =6:18. any person. Heb. nephesh, Jsh +10:28. ver. 15, 30. unawares. Heb. by error. ver. 22, 23. Ex 21:13. Dt 4:42. 19:4, 5.`
