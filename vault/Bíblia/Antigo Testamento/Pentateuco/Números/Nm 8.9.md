`thou shalt bring. Ex 29:4, etc. 40:12. shalt gather the whole. The words kol adath, which are rendered “the whole assembly,” often signify all the elders, or principal persons in the several tribes (Nu 15:4. 25:7. 35:12). And they cannot well have any other sense here; for it would be impossible for all the children of Israel to put their hands on the Levites, as stated in the next verse. shalt gather.
