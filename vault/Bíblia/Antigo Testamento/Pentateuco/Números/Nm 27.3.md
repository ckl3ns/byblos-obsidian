## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Rosenmuller translates this verse as follows: “Our father died in the wilderness, leaving no sons; nor was he among those who rebelled against the Lord with Korah, who died on account of his own sin.” Professor Dathe, however, understands by “his own sin,” that sin which was common to all the Israelites, who died on account of their unbelief. died in the. Nu 14:35. 26:64, 65. Ju 5. in the company. Nu 16:1-3, 19, 32-35, 49. 26:9, 10. died in his. Ezk 18:4. Jn 8:21, 24. Ro 5:12, 21. 6:23.`
