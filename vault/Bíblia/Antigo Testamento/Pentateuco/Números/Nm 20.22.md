## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Kadesh. ver. 1, 14, 16. Nu 13:26. 33:36, 37. Ezk 47:19. 48:28. mount Hor. i.e. who shows, ❅S#2023h. ver. 23, 25, 27. Nu 21:4. 33:37, 38, 39, 41. 34:7, 8. Dt 32:50. (1) A mountain “in the edge of the land of Edom,” Nu 33:37. Edom, or Mount Seir, included the range of mountains which extends nearly from the south end of the Dead Sea to the Gulf of Akabah. About the middle of this range is its highest mountain, which is probably Mount Hor, on which Aaron died, Nu 20:24-29; 33:38, 39; Dt 32:50. (2) A mountain on the northern boundary of the Promised Land. It is mentioned only in Nu 34:7, 8, is probably the extreme northern summit of the Lebanon range, has an elevation of 10,000 feet, and is the highest mountain in Syria.`
