## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`offering. ver. 13, etc. charger. Evidently a large deep bowl for the use of the altar of burnt offerings in the outer court, for all the vessels of the sanctuary were of gold. It was probably used for receiving the flesh of the sacrifices upon which the priests feasted, or the fine flour for the meat offerings. bowl. Mizrak, from zarak, to sprinkle, a bowl or basin, used in sprinkling the blood of the sacrifice (Ex 27:3).`
