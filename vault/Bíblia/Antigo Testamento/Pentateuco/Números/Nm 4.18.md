## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Cut ye not off. Eleazar and the priests would be chargeable with the death of the Kohathites, if they failed to give them proper cautions and directions; or permitted them to gaze with irreverence or curiosity upon the holy things, which they might carry, but not see (ver. 20). Nu 16:32. 17:10. 18:5. Ex 19:21. Le 10:1, 2. 1 S 6:19. 2 S 6:6, 7. Je 38:23.`
