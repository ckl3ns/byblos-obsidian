## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`taskmasters. or, exactors, oppressors. Jb 3:18. Is 3:12. 60:17. Zc 10:4. These taskmasters were Egyptians (Ex 1:11), but the officers were Hebrews. See ver. 14. ver. 10, 13, 15, 19. Ex 1:11. Pr 12:10. officers. Compare Ex 2:14. Nu 11:16. Dt 1:15. 16:18. Jsh 8:33. 24:1, 4. 2 Ch 26:11. They may have been somewhat like the chiefs of trade, where every trade has a head, who is entrusted with authority, and in some measure answerable to the Government. “Officers” (see references) may be evidence of political or governmental organization among the Israelites themselves.`
