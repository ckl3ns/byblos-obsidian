## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`looked. Pr 4:25. Lk 9:62. Ac 7:24-26. this. ƒ84, Ge +6:17. slew. If the Egyptian killed the Hebrew, Moses only acted agreeably to the divine law (Ge 9:6) in thus slaying the Egyptian: nor did he violate the law of Egypt; for, according to Diodorus Siculus (l.1.17), “he who saw a man killed, or violently assaulted on the highway, and did not rescue him if he could, was punished with death.” Moses, therefore, in this transaction, acted as a brave and good man; especially as at this time there was little probability of obtaining justice on an Egyptian murderer. Ge *9:6. Mk ◐=1:25, 26.`
