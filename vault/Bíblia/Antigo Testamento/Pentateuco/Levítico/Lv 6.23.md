## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`shall be. The meat offering of the people was eaten by the priests, who typically bore and expiated their sins; but as no priest, being a sinner, could make atonement for himself, his meat offering must not be eaten, but wholly burnt on the altar, which was a typical transfer of his guilt to the great antitype who actually bore and expiated it. Ex 29:25. He 7:23. it shall not be. ver. 16, 17. Le 2:10.`
