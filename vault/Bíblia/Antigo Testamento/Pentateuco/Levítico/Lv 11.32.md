## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`vessel. Dt +*23:24. it must be put into water. Note that “put into water” means to be rinsed and scoured, Le 6:28n; Dt ◐+23:11. Although Samuel J. Baird identifies this as the single instance of an immersion mentioned in connection with any of the Mosaic ritual (A Bible History of Baptism, p. 136), closer examination of this text and the related passages would point rather to a rinsing, than an immersion. Even if this were conceded to be an instance of immersion, it would be of an object, not a person. See related notes (He ✓9:10n). Le 6:28n. 15:12. Ti 2:14. 3:5.`
