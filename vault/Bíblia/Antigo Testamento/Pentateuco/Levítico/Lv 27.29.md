## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`None. Ex 17:14-16. Nu 21:2, 3. Jsh 6:17-19. 1 S 15:9, 18-23, 26, 33. 1 K 20:42. 1 Co 10:2. which shall be devoted. That is, either that every person devoted to the service of God shall not be redeemed, but die in that devoted state, or, that such as were devoted to death by the appointment and law of God, as the Canaanites were, shall be put to death. of men. i.e. out of men; the reference is to such cases as that of the Amalekites, and the Canaanites, who were thus devoted not by men, but by God out of men, and not at all to the case of a person vowed to the Lord, as in ver. 2-8, who were to be all redeemed, being not devoted, but simply vowed. The original words are quite different. This applies to the case of Jephthah’s daughter (Young). Jg 11:30-40. 21:11. surely. ƒ147B, Ge +2:16.`
