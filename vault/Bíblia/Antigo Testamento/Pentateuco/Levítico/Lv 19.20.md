Young states (Concise Critical Comments) “The original word occurs nowhere else, and has been variously rendered, e.g. gathered, plucked, reproached, by to or for a man.” she shall be scourged. or, they. Le 20:10. Jn ◐8:5, 6. Heb. there shall be a scourging. or, there shall be an investigation. Young states “There is not the slightest ground for the monstrously unjust rendering of the Common (A.V.)
