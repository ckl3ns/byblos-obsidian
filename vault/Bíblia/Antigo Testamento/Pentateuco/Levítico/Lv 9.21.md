## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the breasts. Le 7:24, 26, 30-34. Ex 29:27, 28. Is 49:3. Lk 2:14. 1 P 4:11. wave offering. Le +*23:15. as Moses commanded. The Samaritan text, and thirty MSS. have, kaasher tzivvah yehowah eth Moshe, “as Jehovah commanded Moses;” which is also the reading of the LXX., Arabic, and Targum of Onkelos, and seems to be the true reading, being supported, not only by these authorities, but by the whole chapter itself.`
