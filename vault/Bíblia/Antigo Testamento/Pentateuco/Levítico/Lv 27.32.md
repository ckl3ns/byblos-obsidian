## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`passeth under the rod. The Rabbins say, that when a man gave the tithe of his sheep or calves, he shut them in one fold, in which was a narrow door, to let out but one at a time. He then stood by the door, with a rod dipped in vermilion in his hand, and as they passed he counted them with the rod; and when the tenth came he touched it, by which it was distinguished as the tithe calf, sheep, etc. Young states, “The allusion is to the practice of shepherds, who in counting their flocks caused them to pass by a narrow opening, where with their crooks they could easily number them in succession.” Je 33:13. Ezk 20:37. Mi 7:14. the tenth shall be holy. Nu 18:21. 31:30, 37-41.`
