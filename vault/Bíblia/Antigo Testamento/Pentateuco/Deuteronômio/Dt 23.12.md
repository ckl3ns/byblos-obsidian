## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`a place. lit. hand. Nu 2:17. Ezk 21:19mg. In such a vast camp as that of the Israelites (See notes on Numbers chapter 1 and 2) and indeed, as in every well regulated camp, cleanliness is considered as indispensably necessary. Hence every person who had any disease, and those who were reputed unclean, were forbidden to enter it. Those who have the health of men confided to them, are not ignorant that diseases may easily be produced by putrid exhalations from excrementitious matter. Hence these regulations, though they may appear trifling to some, were essentially necessary to preserve the health of the people.`
