## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`thy heaven. The language here is remarkable: “Thy heaven;” that part of the atmosphere which was over Judea, instead of being replenished with aqueous vapours, should become, with respect to moisture, like brass: and consequently their land would become as hard as iron, and wholly incapable of cultivation; while the clouds might give showers in abundance, and the earth be moist and fruitful in other regions. Ge +9:13. Le 26:19. 1 K 17:1. 18:2. Je 14:1-6. Am 4:6-8. Ja 5:17, 18.`
