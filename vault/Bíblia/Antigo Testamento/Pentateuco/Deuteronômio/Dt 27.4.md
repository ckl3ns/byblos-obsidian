## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`in mount Ebal. The Samaritan text has “in mount Gerizim”; which has given rise to a violent controversy. Dr. Kennicott supposes that the Jews corrupted this passage out of their enmity to the Samaritans, who had their temple on mount Gerizim (Jg 9:7. Jn 4:9, 20); while Dr. Parry and H. Verschuir defend the present reading: to the writings of these authors the reader is referred. Dt 11:29, 30. Jsh 8:30-33.`
