## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`On this side. ver. 47. See on Dt 1:5. 3:29. Nu 32:19. over. Dt 3:29. Beth-peor. Beth-peor was a city which was situated, according to Eusebius, opposite Jericho, and six miles above Livias. As the name signifies the house of Peor, it is probable that there was a temple to Peor, situated in this place, full in view of the people, while Moses was pressing upon them the worship of Jehovah alone; and perhaps the very temple where so many had sinned to their own destruction. Dt 3:29. 34:6. Jsh 13:20. Moses. Dt 1:4. 2:30-36. 3:8. Nu 21:21-32. smote. Ps 136:16-22.`
