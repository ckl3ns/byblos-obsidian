`Esau. ƒ121T3, ƒ121A5, Ge +9:27. Figure of speech Metonymy of Adjunct, whereby “Esau” is put for his posterity. Note that this verse, quoted in Romans 9:13, is often misapplied. The statement given here in Malachi regarding God’s love for Jacob and hatred of Esau was given long after they were born, and applies not to them personally, but to their posterity, as the their histories in Scripture abundantly show (Ezk +35:5, 9n). hated.
