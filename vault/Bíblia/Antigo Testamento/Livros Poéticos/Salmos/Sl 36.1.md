## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`The transgression. Or, rather, “The speech of transgression to the wicked is within his heart: there is no fear of God before his eyes;” for instead of libbi, “my heart,” four MSS. have libbo, “his heart,” which is also the reading of the LXX., Vulgate, Syriac, Arabic, Ethiopic, and Anglo-Saxon. 1 S 15:13, 14. Pr 20:11. Mt 7:16-20. 12:33, 34. Ti 1:16. no. Ps 112:1. Ge 20:11. Pr *8:13. 16:6. Ec 12:13. Ro ▶3:18.`
