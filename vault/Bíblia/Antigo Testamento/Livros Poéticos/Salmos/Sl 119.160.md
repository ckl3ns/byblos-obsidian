## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Thy word is true from the beginning. Heb. The beginning of thy word is true. Dathe renders, “The sum of thy word,” and Abp. Secker, “The principles of thy word;” but the textual rendering, by understanding the preposition mem, “from,” before rosh, “beginning,” appears to be more correct, as it is supported by the following line. T#1026. ver. 86, 89, 90, 138. Pr +*22:21. *30:5. Jn *17:17. 2 Ti *3:16. and every one. ver. 75, 142, *144, 152. Ec *3:14. Mt *5:18. for ever. Heb. olam, Ex +*12:24. SCHIN`
