## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`like rain. T#620. Dt *32:2. 2 S *23:4. Pr 1:23. 16:15. 19:12. Is 5:6. 32:15. *44:3-5. Ezk *34:23-26. 39:29. Ho *6:3. 14:5-7. Jl *2:28, 29. Zc *12:10. 1 P 1:12. mown grass. Heb. gaz, more properly denotes pastured grass; it seems to be a reference to the thick night dews which in the summer fall on the pasturages and become the means of restoring the grass consumed in the day time. This is finely expressed by Virgil (Georg. ii. 201), “And as much as the flocks crop in the long days, so much shall the cold dew restore in one short night.” Is +*26:19n.`
