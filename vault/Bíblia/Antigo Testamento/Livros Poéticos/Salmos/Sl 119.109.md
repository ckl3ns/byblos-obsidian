## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`My soul. Heb. nephesh, Ge +12:13. Rather, “My life (naphshee) is continually in my hand;” i.e. it is in constant danger; every hour I am on the confines of death. The LXX., Syriac, and Ethiopic read “in thy hand;” but this is a conjectural and useless alteration. Jg *12:3. 1 S *19:5. *20:3. Jb *13:14. Ro *8:36. 1 Co 15:31. 2 Co 1:9. *11:23. yet do I not. ver. 61, *83, 93, 117, 141, 153, 176. Ja ◐1:22-25. 2 P 1:12.`
