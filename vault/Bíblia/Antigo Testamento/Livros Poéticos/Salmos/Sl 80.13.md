## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`The boar. The wild boar, chazir, is the parent stock of our domestic hog. He is much smaller but stronger and more undaunted; color, an iron gray, inclining to black; snout, longer than that of the common breed; ears, comparatively short; tusks, very formidable; and habits, fierce and savage. He is particularly destructive to cornfields and vineyards. 2 K ch. 18, 19, 24, 25. 2 Ch ch. 32, 36. Je 4:7. 39:1-3. *51:34. 52:7, 12-14.`
