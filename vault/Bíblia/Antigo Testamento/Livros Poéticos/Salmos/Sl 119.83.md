## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`like a bottle in the smoke. As the bottles in the East were made of skin, it is evident that one of these hung up in the smoke must soon be parched, shrivelled up, lose all its strength, and become unsightly and useless. Thus the psalmist appeared to himself to have become useless and despicable through the exhausted state of his body and mind and by long bodily afflictions and mental distress. Ps 22:15. *102:3, 4. Jb 30:30. yet do I. ver. *16, 61, 176.`
