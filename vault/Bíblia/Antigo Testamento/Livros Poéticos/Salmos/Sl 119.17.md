## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Deal. Gemol, “reward” thy servant: let him have the return of his faith and prayers. From this word is derived the name of the third letter of the Hebrew alphabet, gimmel, which is prefixed to every verse in this part: this is a stroke of the psalmist’s art and ingenuity. ver. 65, 124, 132. Ps 13:6. 116:7. Jn 1:16. 2 Co 9:7-11. Ph *4:19. bountifully. Ps 13:6. *116:7. 1 S +1:22n (✣S#1580h). I may live. Ro 8:2-4. 2 Co *5:14, 15. Ep 2:4, 5, 10. Ti 2:11, 12. 1 J 2:29. 5:3, 4.`
