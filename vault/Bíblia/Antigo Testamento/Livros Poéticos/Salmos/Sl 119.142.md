## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Thy righteousness. Men, as Bp. Horne observes, may decree wickedness by a law or they may change their decrees, and with them what is right today may be wrong tomorrow; but the law of God is righteousness and truth, today and forever. an everlasting. Heb. olam, Ge +*17:7. righteousness. ver. 144. Ps 36:6. Is 51:6, 8. Da 9:24. 2 Th 1:6-10. and thy. ver. 151. Ps *19:9. Jn *17:17. Ep *4:21.`
