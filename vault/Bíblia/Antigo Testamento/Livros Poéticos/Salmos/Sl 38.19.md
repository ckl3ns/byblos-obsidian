## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`But. Ps 3:1. 25:19. 56:1, 2. 59:1-3. are lively, etc. Heb. being living are strong. Instead of chayim, “lively,” Bp. Lowth would read chinnom, “without cause:” “But mine enemies without cause have strengthened themselves.” As this emendation renders this member of the sentence parallel to the other, it is by no means improbable: see Ps 35:19. 79:5. they that. Ps 35:19. 69:4. Mt 10:22. Jn *15:18-25. Ac 4:25-28.`
