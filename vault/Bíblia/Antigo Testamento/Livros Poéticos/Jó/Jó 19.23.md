## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Oh. Heb. Who will give, etc. my words. Jb 31:35. Is 8:1. 30:8. oh that they were. Rather, “Oh that they were described (yuchakoo) in a book, with an iron stile and lead! Were graven on a rock for ever!” Pliny (l. xiii. c. 11) observes, “At first men wrote on palm leaves, and afterwards on the bark or rind of other trees. In the process of time, public monuments were written on rolls of lead (plumbeis voluminibus); and those of a private nature on linen books, or tables covered with wax.”`
