## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the pillars. 1 K 7:21. Ps 87:3. 1 Ti *3:15, 16. Re *3:12. the midst. That is, probably, amatory verses, expressive of the amiable qualities of the bride and bridegroom, wrought either on the coverlet or embroidered carpet. Lady M. W. Montague (let. 29) informs us that the inside even of Turkish coaches are “painted with baskets and nosegays of flowers, intermixed commonly with little poetical mottos.” Ro 5:8. Ep 3:18, 19. Re 1:5. for. Re 21:9-11, 21.`
