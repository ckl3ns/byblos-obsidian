## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`restore. Ex 22:1, 3, 4. 2 S 12:6. Jb *20:18. Lk *19:8. sevenfold. Charles Bridges notes that sevenfold is not used literally. “Four or five-fold was the extent of the Divine requirement. Compare Lk 19:8. It means full (ver. 3) and satisfactory—an indefinite number. Compare Ge 4:15, 24; Ps 79:12, and alia passim. Compare Job 20:18” (Commentary, p. 66). he shall give. Mt *18:25.`
