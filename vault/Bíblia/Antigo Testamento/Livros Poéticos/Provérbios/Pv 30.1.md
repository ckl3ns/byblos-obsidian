## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`Agur. i.e. gatherer, ❅S#94h. Agur was probably a public teacher, and Ithiel and Ucal, his pupils; and this was the massa, or oracle, which he delivered, not by his own wisdom but by the Holy Spirit, for the benefit of man; and which, it is probable, was added by “the men of Hezekiah.” Jakeh. i.e. obedient, ❅S#3348h. even. Pr 31:1. 2 P *1:19-21. Ithiel. i.e. God has arrived; God is with me, ❅S#384h. Pr 30:1, 1. Ne 11:7. (1) One of the children or disciples of Agur, Pr 30:1. (2) A Benjamite, son of Jesaiah, Ne 11:7. Ucal. i.e. prevailing; devoured; I shall be completed, I shall be enabled, ❅S#401h.`
