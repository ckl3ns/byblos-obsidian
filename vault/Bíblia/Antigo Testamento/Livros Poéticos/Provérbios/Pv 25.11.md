## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`word. Pr 15:23. 24:26. Jb 6:25. Ec 12:10. Is *50:4. fitly spoken. Heb. spoken upon his wheels. is like. Rather, “is like golden apples (tappuchim, in Arabic, tuffach) in baskets (maskeeyoth, in Arabic, shakeekat) of silver.” A word fitly spoken with propriety, opportunely, and suitably to the occasion, is as much in its place, and as conspicuously beautiful, as the golden fruit which appears through the apertures of an exquisitely wrought silver basket.`
