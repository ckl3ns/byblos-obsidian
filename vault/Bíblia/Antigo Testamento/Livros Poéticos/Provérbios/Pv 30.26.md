## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`The conies. The shaphan, a creature like a rabbit, though but a feeble animal and incapacitated by its soft, tender feet, from digging, yet, by its sagacity and judgment builds its house in the holes and clefts of the rock; far more inaccessible than the burrows of the rabbit. See on Le 11:5n. Ps 104:18. rocks. Ps 18:2. 91:1, 2. Je 48:28.`
