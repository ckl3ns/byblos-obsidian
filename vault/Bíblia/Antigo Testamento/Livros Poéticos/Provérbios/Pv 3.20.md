## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`the depths. Ge 1:9. 7:11. Jb 38:8-11. Ps 104:8, 9. the clouds. Ge 27:28, 37-39. Dt 33:28. Jb 36:27, 28. 38:26-28. Ps 65:9-12. Je *14:22. Jl *2:23. dew. Dew is defined by Dr. Hutton “a thin, light, insensible mist, or rain, descending with a slow motion, and falling while the sun is below the horizon. It appears to differ from rain as less from more. Its origin and matter are doubtless from the vapors and exhalations that rise from the earth and water.” Like rain, it is wonderfully adapted, by an infinitely wise and gracious Providence, to invigorate and give life to the whole vegetable world. See on Ps 72:6n; Is +*26:19n.`
