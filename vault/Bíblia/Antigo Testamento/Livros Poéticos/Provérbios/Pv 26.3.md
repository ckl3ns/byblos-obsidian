## 🔗 **Referências Cruzadas (NTSK)**

`A whip for. According to our notions, we should rather say a bridle for the horse and a whip for the ass; but it should be considered that the Eastern asses are not only much more beautiful but better goers than ours; and being active and well broken, they need only a bridle to guide them; whereas their horses, being scarce, and often caught wild, and badly broken, are much less manageable, and need the correction of the whip. Pr *10:13. *17:10. *19:25. *27:22. Jg 8:5-7, 16. Ps *32:9. 1 Co 4:21. 2 Co 10:6. 13:2. a rod. T#311. Pr +*13:24 (T#494). 20:8, 30. Dt 13:10, 11. *19:18-20. 2 Ch 9:8. Is 1:26. 1 Ti *1:8-10.`
